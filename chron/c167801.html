Saturday, 1 January 1678, n.s.

22 December 1677, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Thomas Stringer, dated 22 December 1677 o.s. (lost); received (?) 22 Jan 1677/1 Feb 1678. [Journal 1678, page 34]

Person involved: Thomas Stringer (letter)

Wednesday, 5 January 1678, n.s.

26 December 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]

Thursday, 6 January 1678, n.s.

27 December 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 27 December 1677 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; addressed to Locke in Paris; answers a lost letter from Locke dated 12/22 Dec 1677; answered by Locke on 1/15 January 1678. [Corr., i. 365]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Saturday, 8 January 1678, n.s.

29 December 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Cornes [in shorthand], Loosnesse, Diarrhaea, Veneria/Venerea, Salivatio — [Dewhurst]

Letter: From Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 8 January 1678 n.s.; written from Aix; adddressed to Locke at Paris; answers “yours of the 22nd of last month”. [Corr., i. 366]

Person encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter)

Sunday, 9 January 1678, n.s.

30 December 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Palentin, Poison [in French] — [Dewhurst]

Note: Locke dates this day “Mond. Jan. 9”; the entries might have been written on either Jan. 9 or 10.

Tuesday, 11 January 1678, n.s.

1 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Mony, Aspers, Shahees, Sultane, Sherafe, Medine

Saturday, 15 January 1678, n.s.

5 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Vulnus — [Dewhurst]

Letter: To Sir John Banks, dated 15 January 1678 n.s. (draft). [Corr., i. 367]

Persons encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

  ▲  

Monday, 17 January 1678, n.s.

7 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Pay — [Lough]

Letters: To William Charleton, dated 17 January 1678 n.s. (lost); Charleton’s letter of 22 January/1 February answers “yours of the 17th: past”. [See Corr., i. 369]

Person involved: William Charleton (letter)

Thursday, 20 January 1678, n.s.

10 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Relation [repeated on pages 5 and 6], Space [repeated on pages 6, 8, 10, 12, 14 and 16] — [Aaron & Gibb]

Letter: From Dr. John Mapletoft, dated 10 January 1678 o.s.; written from Gresham College, London; answers Locke’s letter of 12/22 December 1677. [Corr., i. 368]

Persons involved: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Friday, 21 January 1678, n.s.

11 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: D Thomas — [Lough]

Saturday, 22 January 1678, n.s.

12 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Memory, Imagination, Madnesse [repeated on pages 19 and 20] — [Aaron & Gibb]

  ▲  

Sunday, 23 January 1678, n.s.

13 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Prejudice, Discourse, Espagne — [Cox, Lough “Reading”]

Monday, 24 January 1678, n.s.

14 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Mercury, C: Northumberland, Sudor diaphoretique, Space — [Dewhurst, Aaron & Gibb]

Wednesday, 26 January 1678, n.s.

16 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Walk — [Lough]

Thursday, 27 January 1678, n.s.

17 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: C: Northumberland — [Dewhurst]

Saturday, 29 January 1678, n.s.

19 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Measures, Pendule, Diarrhaea [repeated on pages 27 and 28] — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Sunday, 30 January 1678, n.s.

20 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Diarrhaea — [Lough, Dewhurst]
