John Locke Chronology

1671

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Friday, 20 January 1671, o.s.

30 January 1671, n.s.

Letter: From [Lord Ashley and] Locke to De Briolay de Beaupreau (draft), dated 20 January 1670/71. [De Beer, i. 250]

Persons encountered: Lord Ashley (letter); De Briolay de Beaupreau (letter)

February

Sunday, 26 February 1671, o.s.

8 March 1671, n.s.

Anthony Ashley Cooper, later third earl of Shaftesbury, born at Exeter House, London.

March

Tuesday, 7 March 1671, o.s.

17 March 1671, n.s.

Letter: To Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland, dated 7 March [1671]; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London; year from Locke’s endorsement. [De Beer, i. 251]

Person encountered: Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland (letter)

Friday, 10 March 1671, o.s.

20 March 1671, n.s.

Letter: To Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. [De Beer, i. 252]

Person encountered: Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland (letter)

  ▲  

April

Saturday, 1 April 1671, o.s.

11 April 1671, n.s.

Letter: To Robert Huntington, dated 1 April 1671; written from Aleppo, Syria; addressed to Locke at Lord Ashley’s in London. [De Beer, i. 253]

Person encountered: Robert Huntington, later Bishop of Raphoe (letter)

May

Friday, 19 May 1671, o.s.

29 May 1671, n.s.

Death of Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland.

Monday, 29 May 1671, o.s.

8 June 1671, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 8h: “Rain.” — [Boyle, 12:79]

June

Monday, 5 June 1671, o.s.

15 June 1671, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 8h: “Cloudy.” — [Boyle, 12:79]

Monday, 16 June 1671, o.s.

26 June 1671, n.s.

Death of Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland.

  ▲  

July

Saturday, 1 July 1671, o.s.

11 July 1671, n.s.

Letter: To Lady Dorothy Ashley, dated 1 [July 1671]; possibly written from Belvoir Castle, where Lady Dorothy’s mother, the Countess of Rutland, was buried on 16 June. addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London; date based on Locke’s endorsement. [De Beer, i. 255]

Person encountered: Lady Dorothy Ashley (letter)

Monday, 10 July 1671, o.s.

20 July 1671, n.s.

Letter: From Mrs. Margaret Blomer, dated 10 July 1671; written from Petworth, Sussex; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. [De Beer, i. 256]

Person encountered: Mrs. Margaret Blomer (letter)

Friday, 14 July 1671, o.s.

24 July 1671, n.s.

Letter: To Margaret Cooper, Lady Ashley, dated 14 July [1671]; written from Petworth, Sussex; year from Locke’s endorsement. [De Beer, i. 257]

Person encountered: Margaret Cooper, Lady Ashley (letter)

Saturday, 15 July 1671, o.s.

15 July 1671, n.s.

Letter: To Lady Dorothy Ashley, dated 15 [July 1671]; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. [De Beer, i. 258]

Person encountered: Lady Dorothy Ashley (letter)

August

Tuesday, 29 August 1671, o.s.

8 September 1671, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 39.

  ▲  

October

Monday, 2 October 1671, o.s.

12 October 1671, n.s.

Letter From Dr. John Mapletoft, 2 October 1671 (missing); answered in Locke’s letter of 10 October. [see De Beer, i. 259]

Person encountered: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Tuesday, 10 October 1671, o.s.

20 October 1671, n.s.

Letter: To Dr. John Mapletoft, dated 10 October 1671; written at Salisbury; addressed to Mapletoft at Mr. Firmin’s, London; answers Mapletoft’s letter of 2 October; possibly answered by Mapletoft, ca. 15 October (missing). [De Beer, i. 259]

Person encountered: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Thursday, 15 October 1671, o.s.

25 October 1671, n.s.

Letter From Dr. John Mapletoft, ca. 15 October 1671 (missing); answers Locke’s letter of 10 October; answered by Locke [27?] October. [see De Beer, i. 259]

Person encountered: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Tuesday, 20 October 1671, o.s.

30 October 1671, n.s.

Locke leaves Salisbury for Sutton [according to De Beer’s speculative reconstruction see De Beer, i. 259]

Friday, 27 October 1671, o.s.

6 November 1671, n.s.

Letter: To Dr. John Mapletoft, 7 [27?] October 1671; misdated, modified date suggested by De Beer; written at Sutton; addressed to Mapletoft at Mr. Firmin’s, London; possibly answers a letter from Mapletoft ca. 15 October. [De Beer, i. 260]

Person encountered: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Uncertain Dates

early summer 1671

Letter: From Sir Peter Colleton, [early summer 1671?]; might have been written in 1670. [De Beer, i. 254]

Person encountered: Sir Peter Colleton (letter)
