1671

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Friday, 20 January 1671, o.s.

30 January 1671, n.s.

Letter dated (draft): From [Lord Ashley and] Locke to De Briolay de Beaupreau. — [De Beer, i. 250]

February

Sunday, 26 February 1671, o.s.

8 March 1671, n.s.

Anthony Ashley Cooper, later third earl of Shaftesbury, born at Exeter House, London.

March

Tuesday, 7 March 1671, o.s.

17 March 1671, n.s.

Letter dated: To Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. — [De Beer, i. 251]

Friday, 10 March 1671, o.s.

20 March 1671, n.s.

Letter dated: To Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. — [De Beer, i. 252]

  ▲  

April

Saturday, 1 April 1671, o.s.

11 April 1671, n.s.

Letter dated: To Robert Huntington, later Bishop of Raphoe, Aleppo, Syria; addressed to Locke in London. — [De Beer, i. 253]

May

Monday, 29 May 1671, o.s.

8 June 1671, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 8h: “Rain.” — [Boyle, 12:79]

June

Monday, 5 June 1671, o.s.

15 June 1671, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 8h: “Cloudy.” — [Boyle, 12:79]

July

Saturday, 1 July 1671, o.s.

11 July 1671, n.s.

Letter dated: To Lady Dorothy Ashley; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. — [De Beer, i. 255]

Monday, 10 July 1671, o.s.

20 July 1671, n.s.

Letter dated: To Mrs. Margaret Blomer, Petworth; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. — [De Beer, i. 256]

Friday, 14 July 1671, o.s.

24 July 1671, n.s.

Letter dated: To Margaret Cooper, Lady Ashley, Petworth. — [De Beer, i. 257]

Saturday, 15 July 1671, o.s.

15 July 1671, n.s.

Letter dated: To Lady Dorothy Ashley; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. — [De Beer, i. 258]

August

Tuesday, 29 August 1671, o.s.

8 September 1671, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 39.

Uncertain Dates

early summer 1671

Letter (undated): From Sir Peter Colleton; undated; might have been written in 1670. — [De Beer, i. 254]

  ▲  

Source: Boyle = The general history of the air, designed and begun by the Honble Robert Boyle Esq; cited from The works of Robert Boyle. Volume 12 (2000), pages 70-89.

De Beer = The correspondence of John Locke / edited by E. S. de Beer (1976-89), 8 vols.
