Saturday, 1 October 1678, n.s.

21 September 1678, o.s.

“From Montaban to Tholose 7 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 304]

Journal entries: Happynesse, Homer — [Goldie, Lough, Lough “Reading”].

Letter: From George Walls, dated 21 September 1678 o.s.; written from Christ Church, Oxford; addressed to Locke through Sir John Banks, London; answers “yours of the 30th of April” and “that from Blois of the 2d of Aug;” (both lost). [Corr., i. 409]

Person encountered: George Walls (letter)

Sunday, 2 October 1678, n.s.

22 September 1678, o.s.

At Toulouse.

Journal entries: Tolose, Virgin, Scalding, Lead — [Lough].

Monday, 3 October 1678, n.s.

23 September 1678, o.s.

At Toulouse.

Journal entries: Lacedemon, Infants, Epilepsia — [Lough “Reading”, Dewhurst].

Thursday, 6 October 1678, n.s.

26 September 1678, o.s.

At Toulouse.

Journal entry: Chartreux — [Lough].

Friday, 7 October 1678, n.s.

27 September 1678, o.s.

At Toulouse.

Journal entries: Plato, Reliques — [Lough “Reading”, Lough].

  ▲  

Sunday, 9 October 1678, n.s.

29 September 1678, o.s.

“From Tolose to Castelnaudary 8 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 309]

Journal entry: Castlenaudary — [Lough].

Monday, 10 October 1678, n.s.

30 September 1678, o.s.

“From Castelnaudary to Carcassone 6 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 309]

Journal entry: Carcassone — [Lough].

Letters:

  • From Sir John Banks, dated 30 September 1678 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; amswers “yours of the 24 instant”. [Corr., i. 410]
  • From Samuel Pepys to Caleb Banks, dated 30 September 1678 o.s.. [Corr., ix. (410A)]

Persons encountered: Caleb Banks (letters), Sir John Banks (letter), Samuel Pepys (letter)

Tuesday, 11 October 1678, n.s.

1 October 1678, o.s.

“From Carcassone to Cavesac [Cabezac] 7 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 309]

Journal entry: Cavesac — [Lough].

Wednesday, 12 October 1678, n.s.

2 October 1678, o.s.

“From Cavesac to Beziers 4 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 309]

Journal entry: Beziers — [Lough].

Thursday, 13 October 1678, n.s.

3 October 1678, o.s.

“From Beziers to Montpellier 11 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 309]

Journal entries: Montpellier, Olive tree — [Lough].

Saturday, 15 October 1678, n.s.

5 October 1678, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 5 October 1678 o.s. (lost); answered by Locke ca. 22 October/1 November. [see Corr., i. 414]

Persons encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

  ▲  

Wednesday, 19 October 1678, n.s.

9 October 1678, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Veterinaria, Cough — [Dewhurst].

Thursday, 20 October 1678, n.s.

10 October 1678, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entry: Botanica — [Lough].

Saturday, 22 October 1678, n.s.

12 October 1678, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Mony, Denarius — [Lough “Reading”].

  ▲  

Monday, 24 October 1678, n.s.

14 October 1678, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Farcy, Gall — [Dewhurst].

Letter: From Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 24 October 1678 n.s.; written from “a la Tour D’aigues”; addressed to Locke at Montpellier. [Corr., i. 411]

Persons encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter)

Thursday, 27 October 1678, n.s.

17 October 1678, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Ulcus, Epilepsia, Surbat — [Dewhurst].

Friday, 28 October 1678, n.s.

18 October 1678, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Letter: From Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 28 October 1678 o.s.; written from “a la Tour D’aigues”; addressed to Locke at Montpellier. [Corr., i. 412]

Persons encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter)

Saturday, 29 October 1678, n.s.

19 October 1678, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entry: Eclipsis lunae — [Lough].

Letters:

  • To Robert Hooke, dated 29 October 1678 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Hooke at Gresham College, London; probably enclosed with a letter to Mapletoft dated 29 October/8 November; encloses an account in French of a lunar eclipse; Locke described the eclipse in his Journal [“Eclipsis lunae”]. [Corr., i. 413]
  • To Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 29 October 1678 n.s. (lost); answered by Locke on 27 Octotber/6 November. [see Corr., i. 416]

Persons encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter), Robert Hooke (letter)

Sunday, 30 October 1678, n.s.

20 October 1678, o.s.

“In the afternoon to Pont Lunell 4 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 319]

Journal entry: Port Lunel — [Lough].

Monday, 31 October 1678, n.s.

21 October 1678, o.s.

“To Nismes 4 leagues. … To Ramoulin 3 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 319]

Journal entries: Nismes, Ramoulin — [Dewhurst].
