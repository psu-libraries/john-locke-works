Lambesc

1676 Apr 10/20: Locke traveled from Aix to Lambesc, and on to Cavaillon.

Lapalud

1675 Dec 20/30: Locke rode from Montélimar to Pont St Esprit, by way of Châteauneuf-sur-Rhône and Lapalud, and on to Orange.

Le Brûlat

1676 Apr 2/12: Locke traveled from Marseilles to Le Brûlat (near Castellet).

Lees, Essex   [Seat of Charles Rich, fourth Earl of Warwick, husband of Mary, sister of Robert Boyle.]

1666 June 2: Letter to Locke from Robert Boyle at Lees.
