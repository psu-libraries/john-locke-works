Abbeville

1675 Nov 21/Dec 1: Locke traveled from Montreuil to Abbeville, where he parted company with Lord Berkeley.

Aignes-Mortes

1676 Mar 14/24: Locke traveled from Montpellier to Pont Lunel, and on to Aigues Mortes.

1676 Mar 27/Apr 6: Locke traveled from Montpellier to Pont Lunel, and on to Aigues-Mortes, Vauvert and St. Gilles.

Aix-en-Provence

1672 Oct 19: Letter from Locke to Dr. John Mapletoft at Aix, where he accompanied Lady Northumberland.

1672 Dec 17/27: Letter to Locke from Mrs. Margaret Blomer at Aix.

1673 Feb 14: Letter from Locke to Dr. John Mapletoft at Aix.

1676 Apr 7/17: Locke traveled from Saint-Maximin to Aix.

1676 Apr 7/17: Locke made notes on the weather at Aix.

1676 Apr 9/19: Locke made notes on the weather at Aix.

1677 Nov 9/19: Letter to Locke from Dr. Denis Grenville at Aix.

1677 Dec 29/1678 Jan 8: Letter to Locke from Dr. Denis Grenville at Aix.

1678 Mar 2/12: Letter to Locke from Dr. Denis Grenville at Aix.

1678 Mar 23/Apr 2: Letter to Locke from Dr. Claude Brouchier at Aix.

1678 Apr 3/13: Letter to Locke from Dr. Denis Grenville at Aix.

1678 Mar 22/Apr 1: Letter to Locke from Dr. Denis Grenville at Aix.

Aleppo, Syria

1671 Apr 1: Letter to Locke from Robert Huntington in Aleppo.

1678 May 22/Jun 1: Letter to Locke from Robert Huntington in Aleppo.

All Souls, Oxford

1660 Oct 23: Letter to Locke from Gabriel Towerson at All Souls.

1661 Apr 9: Letter to Locke from Gabriel Towerson at All Souls

Angers

1668/1669?: Letter from Locke to de Briolay de Beaupreau at Angers.

1678 Aug 10/20: Letter to Nicolas Toinard from Locke at Angers.

1678 Aug 12/22: Letter to de Juigné-Losé from Locke at Angers.

1678 Aug 12/22: Letter from Mme M.-L. Brunyer to Locke at Angers.

Arles

1676 Mar 28/Apr 7: Locke traveled from St. Gilles to Arles.

Arnay-le-Duc

1675 Dec 7/17: Locke traveled from Lucy-le-Bois to Arnay-le-Duc.

Astrop, Northamptonshire

1666 July 9: Dr. David Thomas asks Locke to procure medicinal water from Astrop for Lord Ashley (later Earl of Shaftesbury).

1666 early Aug?: Letter to Locke from Robert Hammond at Astrop.

1666 Aug 16: Letter to Locke from Peter Locke forwarded to Astrop.

1666 Aug 21: Letter to Locke from Charles Perrott forwarded to Astrop.

Auxerre

1675 Dec 6/16: Locke traveled from Joigny to Auxerre and on to Lucy-le-Bois.

Avignon

1675 Dec 21/31: Locke rode from Orange to Avignon.

1676 Apr 11/21: Locke traveled from Cavaillon to Vaucluse, and on to L’Isle-sur-la-Sorgue and Avignon.
