Monday, 8 November 1677, n.s.

29 October 1677, o.s.

At Paris

Journal entries: Acosta, Cunturs, Aune, Ederdons, Verdigrise [not in Locke’s hand; repeated on pages 321, 322, 326, 328, 330, 332, 334, 336], AEs viride [not in Locke’s hand] — [Lough]

The dates in the Journal here become unclear; the essay on Verdigrise ends on page 336; page 337 contains the dates “Tuesday Nov 2” and “Wednes: Nov 3” but the entire page is crossed out. The next date is “Tuesday Nov 9” on page 346. The following entries occur on pages 238-346: Mony, Measures [repeated on pages 340, 342, 343, 344], Weights, Bellonius, Poid, Acosta, Metals, Tomin — [Lough]
