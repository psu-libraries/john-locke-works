Saturday, 1 August 1676, n.s.

22 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Roger, Baer, Pagode [twice], Fanum [twice], Scorpionis ictus — [Lough]

Sunday, 2 August 1676, n.s.

23 July 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Celleneuve: “Thunder, lightening & hard rain the greater part of this morning. Thunder & hard rain a good part of this afternoon.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Quaerenda, Mundus, Weeks — [Lough]

Monday, 3 August 1676, n.s.

24 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Simple ideas, Vintage — [Lough]

Letters received: From Thomas Stringer, dated 15 June 1676 [o.s.] (probably the letter dated 5 June 1676 [o.s.]) and another dated 8 July [o.s.].

Tuesday, 4 August 1676, n.s.

25 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entry: Baucair [i.e. Beaucaire] — [Lough]

Wednesday, 5 August 1676, n.s.

26 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entry: Figues — [Lough]

Thursday, 6 August 1676, n.s.

27 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entry: Grapes — [Lough]

Friday, 7 August 1676, n.s.

28 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entry: Annus

  ▲  

Sunday, 9 August 1676, n.s.

30 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Grapes, Protestants, Catterpiller — [Lough]

Monday, 10 August 1676, n.s.

31 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Leprosie, Mal de vers — [Dewhurst]

Wednesday, 12 August 1676, n.s.

2 August 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Celleneuve: “Thunder & hard shower of 2 howers. 2 or 3 days very hot.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Friday, 14 August 1676, n.s.

4 August 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Wine, Milk

5 August 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Haemorrhoides, Speling [repeated on pages 402 and 404] — [Dewhurst, Von Leyden]

Tuesday, 18 August 1676, n.s.

8 August 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Rheumatism, Renis ulcus, Haemorroids — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Wednesday, 19 August 1676, n.s.

9 August 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Politica, OEconomia, Prudentia 59, Naudé 59

Thursday, 20 July 1676, n.s.

10 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Letter dated: From Andrew Percivall. — [De Beer, i. 317]

Friday, 21 August 1676, n.s.

11 August 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Genii, Virgil, Agrippa

At Celleneuve

14 August 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Faith & Reason, Politica 59 — [Von Leyden]

15 August 1676, o.s.
