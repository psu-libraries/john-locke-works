1684

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Saturday, 1 Jan. 1684, n.s.

22 Dec. 1683, o.s.

[In Amsterdam].

May

Friday, 12 May 1684, n.s.

2 May 1684, o.s.

“From Amsterdam to Harlem 2½ howers. … From Harlem to Heemsted 3m.” — [Journal 1684-85, pages 77-78]

Monday, 15 May 1684, n.s.

5 May 1684, o.s.

“From Heemstede to Amsterdam 10m.” — [Journal 1684-85, page 78]

July

Thursday, 15 July 1684, n.s.

5 July 1684, o.s.

“Removed my lodging to ye Reisers Gracht.” — [Journal 1684-85, page. 94]

Wednesday, 19 July 1684, n.s.

9 July 1684, o.s.

“Removed to Mr Vande Veldes.” — [Journal 1684-85, page 94]

August

Tuesday, 15 Aug. 1684, n.s.

5 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Amsterdam to Alkmar 6 howers.” — [Journal 1684-85, page 107]

Thursday, 17 Aug. 1684, n.s.

7 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Alkmar to Horne by Treckschuyt 3¾ howers … From Hoorn to Enckhuysen 3 howers per wagon.” — [Journal 1684-85, page 108]

Friday, 18 Aug. 1684, n.s.

8 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Enckhuysen to Worcum 4 leagues … From Worcum to Bolswert 2 h. per treckschuyt.” — [Journal 1684-85, pages 110-111]

Saturday, 19 Aug. 1684, n.s.

9 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Bolswert to Franeker 4 l: by sayleing …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 111]

Monday, 21 Aug. 1684, n.s.

11 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Franeker to Leewaerden 3½ per trachschuyt …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 113]

Tuesday, 22 Aug. 1684, n.s.

12 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Leewaerden to Wieuwert 2 h. per trachschuyt …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 114]

Wednesday, 23 Aug. 1684, n.s.

13 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Wiwert to Leewarden 2 h. …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 121]

Thursday, 24 Aug. 1684, n.s.

14 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Leewarden to Doccam 4 h. … From Dockum to Groningen 8 h.” — [Journal 1684-85, page 122]

Saturday, 26 Aug. 1684, n.s.

16 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Groening to Leewarden …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 123]

Thursday, 31 Aug. 1684, n.s.

21 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Leewarden to Sneek 4 h. per tracschuyt …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 126]

September

Saturday, 2 Sept. 1684, n.s.

23 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Sneek to Lemmer 4 l:” — [Journal 1684-85, page 127]

Sunday, 3 Sept. 1684, n.s.

24 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Lemmer to Ens …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 127]

Monday, 4 Sept. 1684, n.s.

25 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Ens to Campen … From thence to Zwolle 3 h. per trackschuyt …” — [Journal 1684-85, pages 127-128]

Wednesday, 6 Sept. 1684, n.s.

27 Aug. 1684, o.s.

“From Zwoll to Deventer per wagen 6 h: …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 128]

Friday, 8 September 1684, n.s.

29 August 1684, o.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 52.

Tuesday, 19 Sept. 1684, n.s.

9 Sept. 1684, o.s.

“From Deventer to Zutphen 2 h: per wagen …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 170]

Wednesday, 20 Sept. 1684, n.s.

10 Sept. 1684, o.s.

“From Zutphen to Arnhem per wagen 5 h: …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 170]

Thursday, 21 Sept. 1684, n.s.

11 Sept. 1684, o.s.

“From Arnhem to Nimegen per wagen 2 h: …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 171]

Friday, 22 Sept. 1684, n.s.

12 Sept. 1684, o.s.

“From Nimegen to Gorcum … 14 hower …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 173]

Saturday, 23 Sept. 1684, n.s.

13 Sept. 1684, o.s.

“From Gorcum to Vianen wagen … 4 h: … From Visanen to Utrech per tracschute 2 h. ” — [Journal 1684-85, page 173]

October

Tuesday, 10 Oct. 1684, n.s.

30 Sept. 1684, o.s.

“From Utrecht to Amsterdam 4 h: …” — [Journal 1684-85, page 203]

Sunday, 15 Oct. 1684, n.s.

5 Oct. 1684, o.s.

“From Amsterdam to Harlem 2½ howers … From Harlem to Leyden per tracschute 4½ howers ” — [Journal 1684-85, page 204]

November

Saturday, 18 Nov. 1684, n.s.

8 Nov. 1684, o.s.

“From Leyden to Amsterdam 7 h:” — [Journal 1684-85, page 227]

December

Saturday, 16 Dec. 1684, n.s.

6 Dec. 1684, o.s.

“From Amsterdam to Utrech: 8 h:” — [Journal 1684-85, page 232]
