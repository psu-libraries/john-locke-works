Sunday, 1 August 1677, n.s.

22 July 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Invalides, Invalids, Villault — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Monday, 2 August 1677, n.s.

23 July 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Ulcera — [Dewhurst]

Letter dated: From Sir John Banks. — [De Beer, i. 346]

Tuesday, 3 August 1677, n.s.

24 July 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Faciei pustulae, Charas, Stringer, Wall, B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Dewhurst, Lough]

Letter received (?): From Thomas Stringer. — [De Beer, i. 344]. The letter is dated l3 July [o.s.]; mentioned in the Journal (see “Stringer” and “Wall” above).

25 July 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter dated: From Dr. John Mapletoft. — [De Beer, i. 347]

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]

Friday, 6 August 1677, n.s.

27 July 1677, o.s.

Journal entries: Pendulum, Ks Library, Printing — [Lough]

Saturday, 7 August 1677, n.s.

28 July 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Measures, Pendulum, Colbert, Levell — [Lough]

Tuesday, 24 August 1677, n.s.

14 August 1677, o.s.

[To Versailles] — [Journal 1677, page 236]

Wednesday, 25 August 1677, n.s.

15 August 1677, o.s.

At Versailles.

Journal entry: Versailes [repeated on pages 236, 238] — [Lough]

Thursday, 26 August 1677, n.s.

16 August 1677, o.s.

[Return to Paris?] — [Journal 1677, page 244]

Journal entries: Versailes [repeated on pages 240, 242], Windemils to raise water — [Lough]

Letters dated: To William Charleton. — [De Beer, i. 350]; from Thomas Stringer. — [De Beer, i. 351]

Stringer’s letter includes a request from Shaftesbury for “the best mapps of Champaigne, Loraigne, Luxenburge, and the Country between the River Sambre and Luxenburg. because the war in probability will come there againe.” Locke purchased the maps on 20/30 September [Journal 1677, page 279] and dispatched them to England on 26 September/2 October.
