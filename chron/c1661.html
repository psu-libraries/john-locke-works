John Locke Chronology

[ Table of Contents ]

1661

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Wednesday, 2 January 1661, o.s.

12 January 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Dr Edmund Meara; addressed to Locke at Pensford. [De Beer, i. 111]

Friday, 4 January 1661, o.s.

14 January 1661, n.s.

Letter written: To “P E.” [Elinor Parry?]. [De Beer, i. 112]

Monday, 8 January 1661, o.s.

18 January 1661, n.s.

Letter written: To Dr Ayliffe Ivye; year supplied based on contents; letter was probably never sent. [De Beer, i. 113]

February

Thursday, 7 February 1661, o.s.

17 February 1661, n.s.

Death of John Locke, sen.

Wednesday, 13 February 1661, o.s.

23 February 1661, n.s.

Burial of John Locke, sen.

Tuesday, 19 February 1661, o.s.

1 March 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Robert Crosse; addressed to Locke at Pensford. [De Beer, i. 114]

March

Tuesday, 12 March 1661, o.s.

22 March 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Gabriel Towerson. [De Beer, i. 115]

April

Tuesday, 2 April 1661, o.s.

12 April 1661, n.s.

Letters to Locke dated: From Mrs Elizabeth Jepp. [De Beer, i. 116]; From John Strachey. [De Beer, i. 117]; both addressed to Locke at Pensford.

Tuesday, 9 April 1661, o.s.

19 April 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Gabriel Towerson; addressed to Locke at [Pensford]. [De Beer, i. 115]

Saturday, 13 April 1661, o.s.

23 April 1661, n.s.

Letter written: To “P E.” [Elinor Parry?]; the year is uncertain. [De Beer, i. 119]

Tuesday, 23 April 1661, o.s.

3 May 1661, n.s.

Coronation of Charles II.

Thursday, 25 April 1661, o.s.

5 May 1661, n.s.

Letter written: To “P E.” [Elinor Parry?]; undated; date suggested by De Beer based on contents. [De Beer, i. 120]

Tuesday, 30 April 1661, o.s.

10 May 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From [Elinor Parry?]; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 121]

May

Tuesday, 14 May 1661, o.s.

24 May 1661, n.s.

Letter dated: From James Allestry, London, to Samuel Tilly, Oxford. [De Beer, i. (123)]

Friday, 17 May 1661, o.s.

27 May 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Dr John Dolben, London; the year is uncertain; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 124]

August

Thursday, 29 August 1661, o.s.

8 September 1661, n.s.

Locke’s twenty-ninth birthday.

October

Thursday, 31 October 1661, o.s.

10 November 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From John Strachey, with a postscript by Elizabeth Jepp; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 125]

December

Sunday, 1 December 1661, o.s.

11 December 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From George Percivall; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 126]

Thursday, 5 December 1661, o.s.

15 December 1661, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Samuel Tilly; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 127]

Undated

Work written [1661?]:   “The preface to the Reader” [for the First tract on government].

Work written (1661):   Essay on infallibility. Dated “61”; this might refer to the year 1661 or to the year 1662 before March 25.

Work written (before 1664; perhaps ca 1661-1662):   “Orozes King of Albania”; dramatis personae and summary of a play.
