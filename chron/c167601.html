Wednesday, 1 Jan. 1676, n.s.

22 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Avignon

Journal entries: Nostre Dame, St. Peter’s, Judaei, Jews, Avignon, Rodanus, Rhosne — [Lough]

Thursday, 2 Jan. 1676, n.s.

23 Dec. 1675, o.s.

[At Avignon]. “Hence we went to Pont du Gard, 4 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 27]

Journal entries: Pont du Gard, Vineae — [Lough]

Friday, 3 Jan. 1676, n.s.

24 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We went from Pont du Gard to Nismes, 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 28]

Journal entries: Nismes [twice], Amphitheaer, Deyron, Pont du Gard, Protestantes, La Religion, Bombicina — [Lough]

Saturday, 4 Jan. 1676, n.s.

25 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We arrived at Montpellier late in the night, having dined at a Protestant Inn at Lunel, 3 leagues from Montpellier.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 35]

Journal entry: Montpellier — [Lough]

  ▲  

Sunday, 5 Jan. 1676, n.s.

26 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Montpellier; “Having horses, we rode out to the seaside, which is a league from the town southward.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 35]

27 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Vectura, Portage, Dinner, Lodging, Horsemeat — [Lough]

Journal entry: Olivae — [Lough]

Journal entries: Walks, Hortus — [Lough]

Journal entries: Kitchin, Lignum, Carbo — [Lough]

Friday, 24 Jan. 1676, n.s.

14 Jan. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “The hils of about 10 or 12 leagues to the N.W. distance coverd with snow, though we had here 4 days continuall hard rain. Soe that winter makes but little excursions into the vale of this country, but keeps his court in the mountains …” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Olivae, Snow, Fuell, Wells, Puteus — [Lough]
