1656

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

February

Tuesday, 14 February 1656, o.s.

24 February 1656, n.s.

[At Oxford?].

Locke was created bachelor of arts. [De Beer, i. 25n.]

Monday, 18 February 1656, o.s.

28 February 1656, n.s.

[At Oxford?].

Letter to Locke dated: From William Godolphin; sent to Locke at Christ Church; dated “Febru: 18 1655[/56]”. [De Beer, i. 25]

October

Saturday, 25 October 1656, o.s.

4 November 1656, n.s.

[At Westminster].

Letter written: To John Locke, sen. [De Beer, i. 29]

November

Saturday, 15 November 1656, o.s.

25 November 1656, n.s.

[At Westminster].

Letter written: To John Locke, sen. [De Beer, i. 30]

December

Wednesday, 10 December 1656, o.s.

20 December 1656, n.s.

Locke was admitted to Gray’s Inn. [De Beer, i. 29n.; The register of admissions to Gray’s Inn / edited by Joseph Foster (1889), p. 280]

Undated

Letter written [summer 1656?]: From Dr. Ayliffe Ivye to a German doctor [De Beer, i. (26)]. There are other versions of this letter, in Latin and English, in Locke’s hand and in (presumably) Ivye’s.

Letter written [summer 1656?]: From Francis Atkins [De Beer, i. 27].

Letter written [summer 1656?]: From John Locke, sen. to an unknown correspondent [De Beer, i. (28)].
