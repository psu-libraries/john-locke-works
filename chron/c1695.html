January

Tuesday, 1 Jan. 1695, o.s.

11 Jan. 1695, n.s.

[At Oates].

Wednesday, 9 January 1695, o.s.

19 January 1695, n.s.

The committee of the House of Commons considering renewal of expiring acts reports and recommend not renewing the Printing Act.

February

Monday, 11 February 1695, o.s.

21 February 1695, n.s.

The House of Commons rejects the committee recommendation against renewing the Printing Act, and called for drafting a new Printing Bil.

March

Monday, 18 March 1695, o.s.

28 March 1695, n.s.

Locke’s proposed amendments to the first 1695 Printing Bill enclosed in a (missing) letter to the College.
