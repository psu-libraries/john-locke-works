January

Wednesday, 1 January 1673, o.s.

11 January 1673, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 22h. — [Boyle, 12:80]

Saturday, 4 January 1673, o.s.

14 January 1673, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 18h. — [Boyle, 12:80]

February

Tuesday, 11 February 1674, o.s.

21 February 1674, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Aubrey, Essex; probably addressed to Locke. — [De Beer, i. 268]

Friday, 14 February 1674, o.s.

24 February 1674, n.s.

Letter dated: To Dr. John Mapletoft, Aix; addressed from Exeter House, London. — [De Beer, i. 269]

April

Thursday, 3 April 1673, o.s.

13 April 1673, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 17h: “Snow.” — [Boyle, 12:80]

May

Saturday, 10 May 1673, o.s.

20 May 1673, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 21h: “Wind E.” — [Boyle, 12:80]

Wednesday, 28 May 1674, o.s.

7 June 1674, n.s.

Letter dated: From Sir Peter Colleton, Barbados; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. — [De Beer, i. 270]

July

Weather observations: “Memorand. That from the Beginning of May, till the middle of July, there was scarce one dry Day, but so great Rains, that produced greater Flouds than were known in the Memory of Man.” — [Boyle, 12:80]

Wednesday, 2 July 1673, o.s.

12 July 1673, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 9h. — [Boyle, 12:80]

18 July 1673, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 10h. — [Boyle, 12:80]

20 July 1673, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 8h: “Fair.” — [Boyle, 12:80]

August

Tuesday, 12 August 1673, o.s.

22 August 1673, n.s.

Letter dated: From Sir Peter Colleton, Barbados; addressed to Locke in London. — [De Beer, i. 275]

Friday, 29 August 1673, o.s.

8 September 1673, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 41.

September

Tuesday, 16 September 1673, o.s.

26 September 1673, n.s.

At Exeter House, London.

Letters dated: To John Strachey, Sutton Court. — [De Beer, i. 276]; from William Allestree, Stockholm; with notes on a picture showing the lives and manners of the Laplanders. — [De Beer, i. 277]

Monday, 29 September 1673, o.s.

9 October 1673, n.s.

Letter dated: From Dr. David Thomas, Salisbury. — [De Beer, i. 278]

October

Thursday, 9 October 1673, o.s.

19 October 1673, n.s.

Letter dated (no longer survives): to William Allestree. — [see De Beer, i. 282]

Wednesday, 15 October 1673, o.s.

31 October 1673, n.s.

Locke sworn secretary of the Council for Trade and Foreign Plantations.

Thursday, 23 October 1673, o.s.

2 November 1673, n.s.

Letter dated: To John Richards, clerk of the Council of Trade and Plantations. — [De Beer, i. 280 (not printed)]

Uncertain date

Letter (undated): To Sir Peter Colleton. — [De Beer, i. 279]

November

Uncertain date

Manuscript: The early history of the Shaftesbury family, [between November 1673 and March 1674].

December

Friday, 26 December 1673, o.s.

5 January 1674, n.s.

Letter dated (no longer survives): To Sir Peter Colleton. — [see De Beer, i. 289]
