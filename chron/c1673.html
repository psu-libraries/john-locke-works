July

August

Tuesday, 12 August 1673, o.s.

22 August 1673, n.s.

Letter: From Sir Peter Colleton, dated 12 August 1673; written from Barbados; addressed to Locke in London. [Corr., i. 275]

Person encountered: Sir Peter Colleton (letter)

Friday, 29 August 1673, o.s.

8 September 1673, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 41.

September

Tuesday, 16 September 1673, o.s.

26 September 1673, n.s.

Letters:

  • To John Strachey, dated 16 September 1673; written from Exeter House, London; addressed to Strachey at Sutton Court. [Corr., i. 276]
  • From William Allestree, dated 16 September 1673; written from Stockholm; with notes on a picture showing the lives and manners of the Laplanders. [Corr., i. 277]

Persons encountered: John Strachey (letter); William Allestree (manuscript)

Monday, 29 September 1673, o.s.

9 October 1673, n.s.

Letter: From Dr. David Thomas, dated 29 September 1673; written from Salisbury. [Corr., i. 278]

Person encountered: Dr. David Thomas (letter)

  ▲  

October

Thursday, 9 October 1673, o.s.

19 October 1673, n.s.

Letter: To William Allestree (lost), dated 9 October 1673; answered by Allestree on 7 January 1673/74. [see Corr., i. 282]

Person encountered: William Allestree (letter)

Wednesday, 15 October 1673, o.s.

31 October 1673, n.s.

Locke sworn secretary of the Council for Trade and Foreign Plantations.

Thursday, 23 October 1673, o.s.

2 November 1673, n.s.

Letter : To John Richards, clerk of the Council of Trade and Plantations, dated 23 October 1673. — [De Beer, i. 280 (not printed)]

Person encountered: John Richards (letter)

Date Uncertain

Letters:

  • From Sir John Nicolas to [Sir Robert Howard], [October 1673]; Early colonial papers. [Corr., i. (278A)]
  • From Sir Peter Colleton, [ca. October 1673]; addressed to Locke in London. [Corr., i. 279]

Persons involved: Sir Peter Colleton, Sir Robert Howard, Sir John Nicholas (letters)

November

Uncertain date

Manuscript: The early history of the Shaftesbury family, [between November 1673 and March 1674].

December

Friday, 26 December 1673, o.s.

5 January 1674, n.s.

Letter: (no longer survives): To Sir Peter Colleton (missing), dated 26 December 1673; answered by Colleton on 22 July 1674. [see De Beer, i. 289]

Person encountered: Sir Peter Colleton (letter)
