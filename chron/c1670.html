January

Tuesday, 10 January 1670, o.s.

20 January 1670, n.s.

Letter: From Margaret Beavis, dated 10/20 January 1670 (lost); answered by Locke on 24 January. [see Corr., i. 241]

Person encountered: Margaret Beavis (letter)

Thursday, 13 January 1670, o.s.

23 January 1670, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 10h; 23h: “Close.” [Boyle, 12:78]

Friday, 14 January 1670, o.s.

24 January 1670, n.s.

Weather observations at London, 10h: “Close.”; 22h: “Mix’d, i.e. Part Clouds, Part clear.” [Boyle, 12:78]

Letter: From Margaret Beavis, dated January 24 1670 n.s.; probably written from Blois; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. Answers Locke’s letter of [December 1669]; answered by Locke on 27 January. [Corr., i. 240]

Person encountered: Margaret Beavis (letter)

August

Tuesday, 23 August 1670, o.s.

2 September 1670, n.s.

Letter: To Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland, dated 23 August 1670; probably answered the countess on 27 August. [Corr., i. 245 (not printed), ix. 245*]

Person encountered: Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland (letter)

Friday, 27 August 1670, o.s.

6 September 1670, n.s.

Letters:

  • From Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland, dated 27 August 1670; probably answers Locke’s letter of 23 August. [Corr., i. 246]
  • To Anthony Ashley Cooper, Lord Ashley, dated 27 August 1670 (lost); answered by Ashley on August 29. [see Corr., i. 247]

Person encountered: Frances Manners, Countess of Rutland (letter); Lord Ashley (letter)

Monday, 29 August 1670, o.s.

8 September 1670, n.s.

Person encountered: Lord Ashley (letter)

Date Uncertain

Letter: From Thomas Blomer, [August 1670]; addressed to Locke at Exeter House, London. [Corr., i. 244]

Person encountered: Thomas Blomer (letter)
