John Locke Chronology

1659

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Monday, 24 January 1659, o.s.

3 February 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: Between unknown correspondents. [De Beer, i. (46)]

February

Friday, 4 February 1659, o.s.

14 February 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 47]

Saturday, 26 February 1659, o.s.

8 March 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 49]

May

Saturday, 21 May 1659, o.s.

31 May 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Locke, sen.; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 54]

Saturday, 28 May 1659, o.s.

7 June 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 55]

June

Saturday, 4 June 1659, o.s.

14 June 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: To an unidentified lady. [De Beer, i. 58]

Wednesday, 22 June 1659, o.s.

2 July 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: To John Locke, sen.. [De Beer, i. 59]

  ▲  

July

Thursday, 7 July 1659, o.s.

17 July 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: From William Godolphin; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 60]

Thursday, 21 July 1659, o.s.

31 July 1659, n.s.

[At Oxford].

Letter dated: To Mrs. Sarah Edwards. [De Beer, i. 64]

August

Monday, 29 August 1659, o.s.

8 September 1659, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 27.

Tuesday, 30 August 1659, o.s.

9 September 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: From Anne Evelegh. [De Beer, i. 71]

September

Monday, 5 September 1659, o.s.

15 September 1659, n.s.

Note dated: A deleted note relating to Henry Stubbe’s An essay in defence of the good old cause. — [British Library, Add. MS. 28,273, f. 12; De Beer, i. 71 n.]

Tuesday, 13 September 1659, o.s.

23 September 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Hoskins. [De Beer, i. 76]

Monday, 19 September 1659, o.s.

29 September 1659, n.s.

[At Pensford].

Letter dated: To an unidentified lady. [De Beer, i. 78]

  ▲  

October

Thursday, 20 October 1659, o.s.

30 October 1659, n.s.

[At Pensford].

Letter dated (written but not sent [?]): To Tom [Westrowe?]. [De Beer, i. 81]

November

Tuesday, 8 November 1659, o.s.

18 November 1659, n.s.

Letters dated (written but not sent [?]): To Tom [Westrowe?]. [De Beer, i. 82]; from Anne Evelegh; addressed to Locke at Pensford. [De Beer, i. 83]

Tuesday, 22 November 1659, o.s.

2 December 1659, n.s.

Letter dated: From Anne Evelegh; addressed to Locke at Pensford. [De Beer, i. 86]

December

Monday, 12 December 1659, o.s.

22 December 1659, n.s.

[At Pensford?].

Letter dated: To an unidentified lady; Locke addressed the letter from Ditcheat, near Pensford, but this may be a whimsical fiction; the year is uncertain. [De Beer, i. 88]

  ▲  

Sources:
De Beer = The correspondence of John Locke / edited by E. S. de Beer. – Oxford : Clarendon Press, 1976-89. – 8 vols. – (Clarendon edition of the works of John Locke)
