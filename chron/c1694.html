1694

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Monday, 1 Jan. 1694, o.s.

11 Jan. 1694, n.s.

[At Oates].

April

Monday, 16 Apr. 1694, o.s.

26 Apr. 1694, n.s.

“Helmont went to London.” — [Memorandum book 1683-1702, page 146]

Monday, 23 Apr. 1694, o.s.

“To Mr Pawlings.” — [Journal 1689-1704, page 8 = 222; Memorandum book 1683-1702, page 146]

July

Tuesday, 3 July 1694, o.s.

13 July 1694, n.s.

“To Winchington.” — [Journal 1689-1704, page 23 = 237]

Wednesday, 11 July 1694, o.s.

21 July 1694, n.s.

“To Mr Pawlings.” — [Journal 1689-1704, page 23 = 237]

Saturday, 14 July 1694, o.s.

24 July 1694, n.s.

“To Oates.” — [Journal 1689-1704, page 24 = 238; Memorandum book 1683-1702, page 146]

  ▲  

August

Wednesday, 29 August 1694, o.s.

8 Sepember 1694, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 62.

September

Wednesday, 19 Sept. 1694, o.s.

29 Sept. 1694, n.s.

“To Mr Pawlings.” — [Journal 1689-1704, page 27 = 241; Memorandum book 1683-1702, page 146]

October

Tuesday, 9 Oct. 1694, o.s.

19 Oct. 1694, n.s.

“To Oates.” — [Journal 1689-1704, page 32 = 246; Memorandum book 1683-1702, page 146]

November

Friday, 30 November 1694, o.s.

10 December 1694, n.s.

The House of Commons appoints a committee to consider renewal of expiring laws, including the 1662 Printing Act.

December

Friday, 7 Dec. 1694, o.s.

17 Dec. 1694, n.s.

“To Mr Pawlings.” — [Journal 1689-1704, page 35 = 249; Memorandum book 1683-1702, page 146]

Saturday, 22 Dec. 1694, o.s.

“To Oates.” — [Journal 1689-1704, page 37 = 251; Memorandum book 1683-1702, page 146]

Date Uncertain

Locke drafts his Criticisms of the 1662 Printing Act [December 1694 or January 1695]

  ▲  

Sources: Journal 1689-1704 = MS. Locke f. 10.

Memorandum book 1683-1702 = MS. Locke f. 29.
