Thursday, 1 December 1678, n.s.

21 November 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Vertue, Pigeon carriers, Azyma, Pascha, Envie — [Lough, Lough “Reading”].

Friday, 2 December 1678, n.s.

22 November 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Horse hoofs, Recreation, Parties, Measures, Scrupulosity — [Dewhurst, King].

There is a gap in the dates in Locke’s journal from 2 December to 5 December. The entries above were written between these dates.

Letter: To Jacques Selapris, dated 2 December 16778 n.s. (lost); answered by Selapris on 6 December. [see Corr., i. 427]

Persons encountered: Jacques Selapris (letters)

Saturday, 3 December 1678, n.s.

23 November 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter:

  • To Thomas Stringer, dated 3 December 1678 n.s. (lost); answered by Stringer on 8/18 December. [see Corr., i. 430]
  • To Sir John Banks, dated 3 December 1678 n.s. (lost); answered by Banks on 16/26 December. [see Corr., i. 433]

Persons encountered: Sir John Banks (letter), Thomas Stringer (letters)

Monday, 5 December 1678, n.s.

25 November 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Salt water, Hydrops — [Lough, Dewhurst].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 25 November 1678 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; addressed to Locke in Paris; answers “your last from Orleans of the 24 November” (lost); answered by Locke on 11/21 December. [Corr., i. 425]

Persons encountered: Sir John Banks (letters)

Tuesday, 6 December 1678, n.s.

26 November 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letters:

  • To Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 6 December 1678 n.s.; answers “yours of 21 Nov”. [Corr., i. 426]
  • From Jacques Selapris, dated 6 December 1678 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers a lost letter from Locke dated 2 December. [De Beer, i. 427]

Person encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter), Jacques Selapris (letter)

Wednesday, 7 December 1678, n.s.

27 November 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Castiglione, People, Arthritis, Claude, Ecclesiasticorum mores, Popes, Roman church — [Dewhurst, Lough “Reading”].

  ▲  

Monday, 12 December 1678, n.s.

2 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Credit, Disgrace — [Goldie].

Tuesday, 13 December 1678, n.s.

3 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Inscription: To Nicolas Toinard; inscribed in Latin on sheets of Toinard’s Evangeliorum Harmonia. [Corr., i. 428 (includes an English translation); H&L 2934]

Person encountered: Nicolas Toinard (letter)

Wednesday, 14 December 1678, n.s.

4 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Possessed, Capucins, Canon, Measures, C.B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough].

Saturday, 17 December 1678, n.s.

7 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: From Thomas Tufton, dated 17 December 1678 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke (by Grenville) in Paris. [Corr., i. 429]

Person encountered: Thomas Tufton, later earl of Thanet (letter)

  ▲  

Sunday, 18 December 1678, n.s.

8 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: From Thomas Stringer, dated 8 December 1678 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “your letter of the 3d of this instant December with mr Selapris letter of Atturney” (lost). [Corr., i. 430]

Person encountered: Thomas Stringer (letters)

Wednesday, 21 December 1678, n.s.

11 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: To Sir John Banks, dated 21 December 1678 n.s.; written at Paris; damaged; answers Banks’s letter of 25 November; answered by Banks on 23 December. [Corr., i. 431]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Monday, 26 December 1678, n.s.

16 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letters:

  • To Nicolas Toinard, dated 26 December 1678 n.s.; addressed to Toinard at Paris. [Corr., i. 432]
  • From Sir John Banks, dated 16 December 1678 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; addressed to Locke in Paris; answers “yours of the 3d” (lost). [Corr., i. 433]

Persons encountered: Sir John Banks (letter), Nicolas Toinard (letter)

Wednesday, 28 December 1678, n.s.

18 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Tussis [in French], King — [Dewhurst, Lough].

Friday, 30 December 1678, n.s.

20 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: 1 John 5.7 — [Lough].

Letter: To Peter Locke, dated 30 December 1678 n.s. (lost); answered by Peter Locke on 22 January/1 February. [See Corr., i. 443]

Person encountered: Peter Locke (letters)

Saturday, 31 December 1678, n.s.

21 December 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Pendule — [Lough].


Date Uncertain

Letter: From an unknown correspondent to Caleb Banks, written in 1678; date based on Locke’s endorsement. [Corr., ix. (437A)]

Person encountered: Caleb Banks (letter)
