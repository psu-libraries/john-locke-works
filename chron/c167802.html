Tuesday, 1 February 1678, n.s.

22 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Review, Muster, Stringer — [Lough]

Letters:

  • From William Charleton, dated 1 February 1678 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke in Paris; answers a lost letter from Locke “of the 17th: past”. [Corr., i. 369]
  • From Thomas Stringer, dated 22 Dec 1677/1 Jan 1678 (lost); received 1 Feb 1678 n.s.. [Journal 1678, page 34]

Persons involved: William Charleton (letter), Thomas Stringer (letter)

Thursday, 3 February 1678, n.s.

24 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 24 January 1678 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; addressed to Locke in Paris. [Corr., i. 370]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Monday, 7 February 1678, n.s.

28 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: King, Dysenteria — [Lough, Dewhurst]

29 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Jumping, Dysentery — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Thursday, 10 February 1678, n.s.

31 January 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Algebra, Dysentery, Water in vacuo, Mapletoft — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Friday, 11 February 1678, n.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Dysentery — [Dewhurst]
