Balaruc-les-Bains

1676 Feb 23/Mar 4: Locke rode from Montpellier to Balaruc-les-Bains.

Barbados

1673 May 28: Letter to Locke from Sir Peter Colleton at Barbados.

1673 Aug 12: Letter to Locke from Sir Peter Colleton at Barbados.

1674 Mar 3: Letter to Locke from Sir Peter Colleton at Barbados.

1674 July 22: Letter to Locke from Sir Peter Colleton at Barbados.

Beaumont

1675 Nov 24/Dec 4: Locke traveled from Tillart to Beaumont and on the Paris.

Beauvais

1675 Nov 23/Dec 3: Locke traveled from Poix to Beauvais and on to Tillart.

1669 Aug 29: Letter from Lord Ashley to Locke at Belvoir Castle.

1669 Sep 6: Letter from Lord Ashley to Locke at Belvoir Castle.

1659 Nov 8: Letter to Locke from Anne Evelegh at Black Hall, Oxford.

Boulogne

1675 Nov 19/29: Locke traveled from Calais to Boulogne.
