Tuesday, 1 June 1677, n.s.

22 May 1677, o.s.

“From Orleance to Tours 10 leagues. … From Tours to Estamps 10 leagues.” — [Journal 1677, page 149]

Journal entries: Tours, Estamps — [Lough]

Wednesday, 2 June 1677, n.s.

23 May 1677, o.s.

“From Estampes to Chatres 6 leagues. From Chatres to Paris 8 leagues.” — [Journal 1677, page 149]

Journal entries: Chatres, Chily, Seau, B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Tumidum corpus, Paralysis, Naevi — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Thursday, 3 June 1677, n.s.

24 May 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Merces, Villault, Marchandise — [Lough]

Friday, 4 June 1677, n.s.

25 May 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Letters:

  • To Robert Boyle, dated 4 June 1677 n.s.; written from Paris. [Corr., i. 335]
  • To Sir John Banks, 4 June 1677 n.s. (lost); answered by Banks on 5/15 June 1677. [See Corr., i, 338]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter), Robert Boyle (letter)

Wednesday, 9 June 1677, n.s.

30 May 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Stringer, Coaches — [Lough]

Letter: To Sir John Banks, dated 9 June 1677 n.s.; written from Paris; addressed to Banks at Lincolns Inn Fields, London; answers Bank’s letters of 2/12 April 1677 and 3/13 May 1677; answered by Banks on 14/24 June 1677 and 18/28 June 1677. [Corr., i. 336]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Saturday, 12 June 1677, n.s.

2 June 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Stringer, B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Sr Jo: Banks — [Lough]

  ▲  

Sunday, 13 June 1677, n.s.

3 June 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks; twice] — [Lough]

Monday, 14 June 1677, n.s.

4 June 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]

Letter: From Dr. Thomas Sydenham, dated 4 [June] 1677 o.s.; month based on Locke’s endorsement; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 337]

Person encountered: Dr. Thomas Sydenham (letter)

Tuesday, 15 June 1677, n.s.

5 June 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 5 June 1677 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers a lost letter dated 25 March/4 June 1677. [Corr., i. 338]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Wednesday, 16 June 1677, n.s.

6 June 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]

Thursday, 17 June 1677, n.s.

7 June 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Goblins, B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Winde, Foode, Aire, Puerperium, Infantes, Appetites, Wine of palmes, Ossat, Cypher, Delineating parallelogram, Designeing — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Sunday, 20 June 1677, n.s.

10 June 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Silver melt, Vermes aurium, Oculorum inflammation, Observatoire, Goiters, Cancer, Strumae, Quartan — [Lough, Dewhurst]

  ▲  

Tuesday, 22 June 1677, n.s.

12 May 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: To Dr. John Mapletoft, dated [12/]22 June 1677 n.s.; written from Paris; addressed to Mapletoft at Gresham College, London; answered by Mapletoft on 25 July/4 August 1677. [De Beer, i. 339]

Person encountered: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Wednesday, 23 June 1677, n.s.

13 May 1677, o.s.

“From Paris to Versales 4 leagues.” — [Journal 1677, page 169]

Journal entries: Versailes [repeated on pages 169, 170, 172], B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]

Thursday, 24 June 1677, n.s.

14 May 1677, o.s.

[Return to Paris]

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 14 June 1677 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers Locke’s letter of 30 May/9 June 1677. [Corr., i. 340]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Friday, 25 June 1677, n.s.

15 May 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Wines Artificial [in French; repeated on pages 173, 174, 176; continued on 1 July], — [Lough]

Sunday, 27 June 1677, n.s.

17 May 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]

Monday, 28 June 1677, n.s.

18 May 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Jacobines — [Lough]

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 18 June 1677 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; answers Locke’s letter of 30 May/9 June 1677. [Corr., i. 341]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Tuesday, 29 June 1677, n.s.

19 May 1677, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]
