November

Friday, 12 Nov. 1675, o.s.

22 Nov. 1675, n.s.

“In the evening I went to Gravesend.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 1]

Sunday, 14 Nov. 1675, o.s.

24 Nov. 1675, n.s.

“Took water at Dover and that day arrived at Calais.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 1]

Friday, 29 Nov. 1675, n.s.

19 Nov. 1675, o.s.

“Went to Boulogne, 7 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 1]

Saturday, 30 Nov. 1675, n.s.

20 Nov. 1675, o.s.

“To Montreuil, 7 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 1]

December

Sunday, 1 Dec. 1675, n.s.

21 Nov. 1675, o.s.

“To Abbeville, 10 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 1]

Monday, 2 Dec. 1675, n.s.

22 Nov. 1675, o.s.

“To Poix, 9 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 1]

Tuesday, 3 Dec. 1675, n.s.

23 Nov. 1675, o.s.

“To Beauvais, 9 leagues, and thence to Tillart, 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 1]

Wednesday, 4 Dec. 1675, n.s.

24 Nov. 1675, o.s.

“To Beaumont, 5 leagues, thence to Paris, 8 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 1]

Saturday, 14 Dec. 1675, n.s.

4 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We took coach at Paris in the diligence for Lyons and, after having dined at Melun, got by an hour or 2 in the night to Moret where we lodged. It is 16 leagues from Paris.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 2]

Sunday, 15 Dec. 1675, n.s.

5 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“Dind at Pont sur Yonne & reached Joyny [Joigny], 17 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 2]

Monday, 16 Dec. 1675, n.s.

6 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We dined at Auxerre … We lay at Lacie le Bois [Lucy-le-Bois], 15 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 2]

Tuesday, 17 Dec. 1675, n.s.

7 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We lay at Arnan le Duc [Arnay-le-Duc], 15 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 3]

Wednesday, 18 Dec. 1675, n.s.

8 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We dind at a litle town cald Chany [Chagny], 8 Leagues … Lay at Challon [Châlons-sur-Saône], 3 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 3]

Thursday, 19 Dec. 1675, n.s.

9 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We took boat at Challon on the Saone … We passed by Tornus [Tournus] … 5 Leagues from Tornus we came to Mascon where we lay.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 4-5]

Friday, 20 Dec. 1675, n.s.

10 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We enterd our boat again and at 5 leagues’s distance came to a pretty towne calld Bel-Ville … A league from Belville we came to Ville Franche … Two Leagues farther we came to Trevou [Trêvoux] … From thence to Lyons, 3 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 5-6]

Thursday, 26 Dec. 1675, n.s.

16 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“[W]e took horse & rode to Vienne, 5 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 17]

Friday, 27 Dec. 1675, n.s.

17 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We went 7 leagues to St. Valer [Saint-Vallier] …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 18]

Saturday, 28 Dec. 1675, n.s.

18 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“To Valence, 7 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 18]

Sunday, 29 Dec. 1675, n.s.

19 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“To Montelimar, 8 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 19]

Monday, 30 Dec. 1675, n.s.

20 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“From Montelimar to Pon[t] St. Esprit, 5 leagues [via Chasteauneuf and Pallu [Lapalud]]. 3 leagues from Pont St. Esprit we came to Orange …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 19-22]

Tuesday, 31 Dec. 1675, n.s.

21 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We came to Avignon, 4 leagues from Orange …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 24]
