Wednesday, 1 April 1676, n.s.

22 March 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Mensura, Guis, Amphitheatrum — [Lough]

Friday, 3 April 1676, n.s.

24 March 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Papae, Viola, Moneta — [Lough]

  ▲  

Easter Sunday, 5 April 1676, n.s.

26 March 1676, o.s.

Weather observations near Port Lunell: “[Wind] S. Fair. Very hot.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Monday, 6 April 1676, n.s.

27 March 1676, o.s.

“To Pont Lunell to diner, 4 leagues. From thence over the valy of Aigues Mortes, 2 leagues … At Bauvair [Vauvert] … To St. Giles 2 leagues more …” — [Journal 1675-76, pages 180-181]

Journal entries: Bauvair, Protestantes [twice], St. Gyles, Ovis, Deus pater, Chorea — [Lough]

Tuesday, 7 April 1676, n.s.

28 March 1676, o.s.

“From St. Giles to Arles 3 leagues.” — [Journal 1675-76, page 182]

Journal entries: Arles [repeated on pages 182, 184-187], Camargue, Obeliscus, Coemeterium, Mare, Amphitheatrum — [Lough]

Wednesday, 8 April 1676, n.s.

29 March 1676, o.s.

“From Arles to St. Martins to dinner. 3 leagues to St. Martin de Crau. … From St. Martin to Salon to bed, 4 leagues …” — [Journal 1675-76, pages 189-190]

Journal entries: Arles [repeated on pages 188-189], Deus pater, St. Martin, Crau, Salon, Nostradamus — [Lough]

Thursday, 9 April 1676, n.s.

30 March 1676, o.s.

“From Sallon to the Griffon, 5 leagues … From the Griffon to Marseilles 3½ leagues …” — [Journal 1675-76, page 194]

Journal entries: Marseilles — [Lough]

Friday, 10 April 1676, n.s.

31 March 1676, o.s.

At Marseilles.

Journal entries: Marseilles [repeated on pages 194, 196-197], Triremis, Dieta, Arsenal, Sal — [Lough]

Saturday, 11 April 1676, n.s.

1 April 1676, o.s.

At Marseilles.

Journal entries: Marseilles, Batallion — [Lough]

  ▲  

Sunday, 12 April 1676, n.s.

2 April 1676, o.s.

“We left Marseilles … and lay that night at Maison brulée [Le Brûlat], a single house near Castilet, 6 leagues.” — [Journal 1675-76, page 198]

Journal entries: Maison brul√©e, Murus, Terebinthus, Bois de Conu, Agricultura — [Lough]

Monday, 13 April 1676, n.s.

3 April 1676, o.s.

“From Maison brullé to Toulon, 3½ leagues …” — [Journal 1675-76, page 200]

Journal entries: Toulon/Thoulon [repeated on pages 201-203], Ventus, Navis, Arsenal, Hortus — [Lough]

Tuesday, 14 April 1676, n.s.

4 April 1676, o.s.

“From Toulon to Hyeres, 3 leagues to bed.” — [Journal 1675-76, page 204]

Journal entries: Antlia, Hyeres [repeated on pages 204, 206], Aurantia, Coquinaria, Mony — [Lough]

Wednesday, 15 April 1676, n.s.

5 April 1676, o.s.

“From Hyeres to Maun [Méounes] to dinner, 4 leagues … 2 leagues from Hyeres we came to Solier [Solliès] … From thence to Beaugency [Belgentier?], 1 league … From Meaunes to St. Baum, 3 leagues …” — [Journal 1675-76, pages 206, 209]

Journal entries: Maun, Solier, Staecas, Beaugency, Ficus Americanus, St Baum — [Lough]

Thursday, 16 April 1676, n.s.

6 April 1676, o.s.

“From St. Baume to St. Maximin, 3 leagues.” — [Journal 1675-76, page 210]

Weather observations at St. Maximin: “Cloudy, cold.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: St. Maximin, Quarterings — [Lough]

Letter dated: From Thomas Stringer. — [De Beer, i. 311]

Friday, 17 April 1676, n.s.

7 April 1676, o.s.

“From St. Maximin to Aix, 6 leagues …” — [Journal 1675-76, page 210]

Weather observations at Aix: “[Wind] N.E. Fair, cold.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Aix, Aurantia, Inoculateing, Women rideing — [Lough]

Saturday, 18 April 1676, n.s.

8 April 1676, o.s.

At Aix.

Journal entry: Musaea — [Lough]

  ▲  

Sunday, 19 April 1676, n.s.

9 April 1676, o.s.

At Aix.

Weather observations at Aix: “Fair & cold in the shade.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Chlorosis, Quartana, Carnositas, Venerea, Processions, Sicamores de malt, Genista fl. albo, Aix, Acidulae artificiales — [Dewhurst, Lough]

Letter (date uncertain): From Dr. Claude Brouchier. — [De Beer, i, page 451n.]

Monday, 20 April 1676, n.s.

10 April 1676, o.s.

“From Aix to Lambesc, 3 leagues … From Lambesc to Cavaillon, 4 leagues.” — [Journal 1675-76, page 218]

Weather observations at Cavaillon: “[Wind] N.W. Fair, cold.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Lambesc, Durance, Cavaillon — [Lough]

Tuesday, 21 April 1676, n.s.

11 April 1676, o.s.

“From Cavaillon to Vaucluse, 2 leagues … From Vaucluse to Lisle [L’Isle-sur-la-Sorgue], 1 league, from Lisle to Avignon, 4 leagues …” — [Journal 1675-76, pages 219-220]

Journal entries: Vaucluse, Avignon, Mony, Pastas — [Lough]

Wednesday, 22 April 1676, n.s.

12 April 1676, o.s.

“At Ville Neuf over against Avignon on the other side [of] the Rhosne … From Avignon to Tarascon, 4 leagues …” — [Journal 1675-76, pages 221-222]

Journal entries: Ville neuf, Carthusians [twice], Avignon Walls, Vectigal, Mony — [Lough]

Thursday, 23 April 1676, n.s.

13 April 1676, o.s.

“From Tarascon to Generat [Générac], 4 leagues, & from Generat to Pont Lunel 4 leagues.” — [Journal 1675-76, page 226]

Journal entries: Tarascon, Provence, Mony, Beaucaire — [Lough]

Friday, 24 April 1676, n.s.

14 April 1676, o.s.

“From Pont Lunel to Montpellier, 4 leagues.” — [Journal 1675-76, page 229]

Saturday, 25 April 1676, n.s.

15 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Rates, Pretium — [Lough]

  ▲  

Tuesday, 28 April 1676, n.s.

18 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Liguoris, Colica — [Lough]

Wednesday, 29 April 1676, n.s.

19 April 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Cavaillon: “Snow on the hills beyond Peir St. Loup.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Thursday, 30 April 1676, n.s.

20 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Sapo, Soap — [Lough]
