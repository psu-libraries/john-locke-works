Friday, 1 October 1677, n.s.

21 September 1677, o.s.

At Paris

Journal entries: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Sensation, Delight, Senses, Monsieur — [Lough, Aaron & Gibb]

Saturday, 2 October 1677, n.s.

22 September 1677, o.s.

At Paris

“Sent the maps by Mrs. Armstrong.” [Journal 1677, page 286] This refers to maps requested by Shaftesbury on 16/26 August and purchased by Locke on 20/30 September. Mrs. Armstrong is the daughter of Sir Thomas Armstrong.

Sunday, 3 October 1677, n.s.

23 September 1677, o.s.

Weather observations at Paris, “Very faire & warme as it was almost all September.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Journal entries: America, Mony

Monday, 4 October 1677, n.s.

24 September 1677, o.s.

At Paris

Journal entries: Menstrua, Venerea, Library [the King’s Library], Atlantis, B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Dewhurst, Lough, Bellatalla]

Wednesday, 6 October 1677, n.s.

26 September 1677, o.s.

“At Versailles.” — [Journal 1677, page 289]

Journal entries: Versailles, Garden — [Lough]

Letter (missing): To Thomas Stringer; mentioned in Stringer’s letter of 5/15 October.

Thursday, 7 October 1677, n.s.

27 September 1677, o.s.

At Paris (?)

Journal entries: Coach, Palimpsestus, Peau d’asne, Esselhaut, Maps, Table book, Observatoire, Declination — [Lough]

Friday, 8 October 1677, n.s.

28 September 1677, o.s.

At Paris (?)

Journal entries: Table book, Indostan, Antiquity, Arithmatique, Weeks — [Lough]

Saturday, 9 October 1677, n.s.

29 September 1677, o.s.

At Paris (?)

Journal entries: Esselhaut, St Geneveve, As, Mony, Denarius, Quinarius, Sestertius, Congius, Sextarius — [Lough]
