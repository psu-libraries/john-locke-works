Wednesday, 1 Febuary 1679, n.s.

22 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Peter Locke, dated 22 January 1679 o.s.; written from Sutton; answers “yours of 30 dec:”. [Corr., i. 434]

Person encountered: Peter Locke (letter)

Thursday, 2 February 1679, n.s.

23 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 23 January 1679 o.s.; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 444]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Friday, 3 February 1679, n.s.

24 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: To Jacques Selapris, dated 3 February 1679 n.s. (lost); answered by Selapris on 2/12 March 1679. [See Corr., i. 451]

Person encountered: Jacques Selapris (letter)

Tuesday, 14 February 1679, n.s.

4 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From William Charleton, dated 14 February 1679 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke at Paris; with enclosures: (1) a catalogue of seeds; (2) an inventory of “things sent from Montpellier 14 Feb. 79”. [Corr., i. 445]

Person encountered: William Charleton (letter)

Sunday, 19 February 1679, n.s.

9 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Jacques Horutener and Jacques Selapris, dated 19 February 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 446]

Person encountered: Jacques Horutener (letter), Jacques Selapris (letter)

Monday, 20 February 1679, n.s.

10 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 20 February 1679 n.s.; written from La Tour d’Aigues; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “yours of Jan. the 27th” (lost). [Corr., i. 447]

Persons encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter)

Tuesday, 21 February 1679, n.s.

11 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Thomas Tufton, dated 21 February 1679 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “your last letter dated the 27th of jannuary” (lost). [Corr., i. 448]

Persons encountered: Thomas Tufton, later Earl of Thanet (letter)

Tuesday, 28 February 1679, n.s.

18 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From William Charleton, dated 28 February 1679 n.s.; written from Montpellier; with enclosures: (1) a catalogue of grapes; (2) a note from Dr. Pierre Magnol, in French.. [Corr., i. 449]

Persons encountered: William Charleton (letter), Dr. Pierre Magnol (enclosed note)
