Friday, 1 Jan. 1677, n.s.

22 Dec. 1676, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Saturday, 2 Jan. 1677, n.s.

23 Dec. 1676, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entries: Arabia, Scheriffi, Diadorus, Aegypt

Sunday, 3 Jan. 1677, n.s.

24 Dec. 1676, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entries: Geraldinus, Africa, Images

Monday, 4 Jan. 1677, n.s.

25 Dec. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Hard snow for 3 or 4 hours.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entry: Genebrardo

Tuesday, 5 Jan. 1677, n.s.

26 Dec. 1676, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entries: Picherellus, Ligorius, Strabo, Aegyptian worship, Sitis, States [Estates of Languedoc], Salt — [Lough]

Wednesday, 6 Jan. 1677, n.s.

27 Dec. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Fair, very hard frost.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Thursday, 7 Jan. 1677, n.s.

28 Dec. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Fair, very hard frost.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Journal entries: Rain, Sahid, Bellonia, Gummi Arabicum

Friday, 8 Jan. 1677, n.s.

29 Dec. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Fair, freeze in my chamber.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Saturday, 9 Jan. 1677, n.s.

30 Dec. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Freez in my chamber.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Oculi debiles, Dentium dolor — [Dewhurst]

  ▲  

Sunday, 10 Jan. 1677, n.s.

31 Dec. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Fair. [Wind] N. Freez hard in my chamber.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entry: Vulnera — [Dewhurst]

Monday, 11 Jan. 1677, n.s.

1 Jan. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Freez hard in my chamber.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Seeds, Hogs, Cheez — [Lough]

Tuesday, 12 Jan. 1677, n.s.

2 Jan. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Freez in my chamber in the night.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Ethiopi, Zibethum, Moloch, Remphan, Maidin, Thuanus, No√ºe

Journal entries: Funerall, Davila, Italians, Opoponax

Saturday, 16 Jan. 1677, n.s.

6 Jan. 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “Cloudy, very warm.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; shorthand]
