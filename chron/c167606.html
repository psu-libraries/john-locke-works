Monday, 1 June 1676, n.s.

22 May 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Kermes, Eggs — [Lough]

Tuesday, 2 June 1676, n.s.

23 May 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “Very warme, but since the rain that fell some days since, it was very cool till now.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Silkwormes/Silkworms [repeated on pages 264, 267-268], Cremot Tartar, Apoplexie, Rheumatisme — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Wednesday, 3 June 1676, n.s.

24 May 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “Very hot. At sunset great lightening in several flashes. There seemed great streaks of fire in the sky. Some thunder at a distance and a very little shower of rain.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; shorthand]

Journal entry: Edict of Nantes — [Lough]

Thursday, 4 June 1676, n.s.

25 May 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Silk wormes [repeated on pages 270-271], Feste dieu [repeated on pages 271-272] — [Lough]

Friday, 5 June 1676, n.s.

26 May 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Ophthalmia, Gardiner — [Dewhurst, Lough]

Saturday, 6 June 1676, n.s.

27 May 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entry: Sesterce

29 May 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Silkwormes, Kermes, Eeles, Lapis Lazuli, Civet, Nicephor, Gunpouder plot — [Lough]

30 May 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entry: Silke worme — [Lough]

Journal entry: Eclyps — [Lough]

2 June 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Saturday, 13 June 1676, n.s.

3 June 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entry: Measures

  ▲  

Sunday, 14 June 1676, n.s.

4 June 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Bezoar, Crurum tumor, Dying, Essaye, Indigo, Protestants — [Dewhurst, Lough]

Monday, 15 June 1676, n.s.

5 June 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Seeds, Conservatio corporum

Letter dated: From Thomas Stringer; probably the letter of “15” June mentioned on August 3. — [De Beer, i. 312]

Tuesday, 16 June 1676, n.s.

6 June 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Silkworms, Kermes, Burial, Ascensus liquorum — [Lough]

Wednesday, 17 June 1676, n.s.

7 June 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entry: Silkworms — [Lough]

Thursday, 18 June 1676, n.s.

8 June 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Gutta serena, Linin, Hysterica, Epilepsia, Guibert, Prevot — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Friday, 19 June 1676, n.s.

9 June 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “[Wind] N.W. Clouds & some few drops of rain & soe coole these 3 last days that the balls that had swam without any sinking for severall weeks, now sank again.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Journal entry: Purgans

Saturday, 20 June 1676, n.s.

10 June 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Extension, Extensio [repeated on pages 290, 292, 294] — [Aaron & Gibb]

  ▲  

Sunday, 21 June 1676, n.s.

11 June 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “Faire & soe hot that the ball L which had sank for the 3 or 4 days last past in the Fountain Putanel, rose again this evening and swam.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Journal entry: Silkworms — [Lough]

Tuesday, 23 June 1676, n.s.

13 June 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “Fair & very hot so that my 2 balls L and I rose again in the Fountain Putanel.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Journal entries: Bonfires, Silkworms, Dying, Nobility — [Lough]

Thursday, 25 June 1676, n.s.

15 June 1676, o.s.

“Went to Selneuf [Celleneuve] to Mr. Michard’s.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 302; Locke took lodgings for the summer outside of Montpellier]

Journal entry: Bishops funeral — [Lough]

Saturday, 27 June 1676, n.s.

17 June 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Land, Taxes — [Lough]

Sunday, 28 June 1676, n.s.

18 June 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entries: Testamenti Novi harmonia, Harvest, Vineards — [Lough]
