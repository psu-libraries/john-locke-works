Friday, 5 August 1678, n.s.

26 July 1678, o.s.

“We saw Menars, a fine house … situated about 2 leagues above Bloys … We also say Chambort … about 3 leagues from Bloys … We also saw Beauregard, a litle house … about 1½ leagues from Bloys.” — [Journal 1678, pages 233-236]

Monday, 8 August 1678, n.s.

29 July 1678, o.s.

“From Bloys to Tours 16 leagues …” — [Journal 1678, page 241]

Wednesday, 10 August 1678, n.s.

31 July 1678, o.s.

“From Tours to Verret 2 leagues … [returned to Tours]” — [Journal 1678, page 243]

Friday, 12 August 1678, n.s.

2 August 1678, o.s.

“From Tours to Saumur 17 leagues. cariage by water in a boat by our selves …” — [Journal 1678, page 252]

Friday, 19 August 1678, n.s.

9 August 1678, o.s.

“From Saumur to Angers 10 leagues …” — [Journal 1678, page 256]

Friday, 26 August 1678, n.s.

16 August 1678, o.s.

“We saw the quarrys of Ardoise.” — [Journal 1678, page 264]

Monday, 29 August 1678, n.s.

19 August 1678, o.s.

“From Angers to Saumur 10 litle leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 270]

Tuesday, 30 August 1678, n.s.

20 August 1678, o.s.

“From Saumur to Chinon 6 leagues. … From Chinon to Richelieu 4 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, pages 271-272]

Wednesday, 31 August 1678, n.s.

21 August 1678, o.s.

“From Richleiue [sic] we went to Milleray [La Meilleraye] 11 or 12 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 275]
