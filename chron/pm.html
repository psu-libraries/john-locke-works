Mâcon

1675 Dec 9/19: Locke traveled by boat from Châlons-sur-Saône to Tournus and on to Mascon.

Marseilles

1676 Mar 30/Apr 9: Locke traveled from Salon to the Griffon, and on to Marseilles.

Melun

1675 4/Dec 14: Locke traveled from Paris to Melun and on to Moret.

Méounes

1676 Apr 5/15: Locke traveled from Hyères through Solliès and Belgentier [?] to Méounes, and on to Sainte-Baum.

Montélimar

1675 Dec 19/29: Locke rode from Valence to Montélimar.
