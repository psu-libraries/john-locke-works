Monday, 1 Feb. 1677, n.s.

22 Jan. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entry: Spanish Lady — [Lough]

Thursday, 3 Feb. 1677, n.s.

24 Jan. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entry: Verses — [Lough]

Friday, 4 Feb. 1677, n.s.

25 Jan. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entries: Venerea, Salivatio, Ascanides, Convulsio, Nun professed — [Dewhurst, Lough]

Monday, 8 Feb. 1677, n.s.

29 Jan. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entries: Understanding, Knowledg its extent & measure, End of knowledge, Knowledg [repeated on pages 48, 48, 50, 52] — [Aaron & Gibb]

Wednesday, 10 Feb. 1677, n.s.

31 Jan. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entry: Burthens — [Lough]

1 Feb. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entry: Tertiana

Friday, 12 Feb. 1677, n.s.

2 Feb. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entries: Arguments positive & negative, Understanding — [Aaron & Gibb]

Saturday, 13 Feb. 1677, n.s.

3 Feb. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entry: Q Hungarys water — [Lough]

Wednesday, 17 Feb. 1677, n.s.

7 Feb. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entry: Purge — [Dewhurst]

Friday, 19 Feb. 1677, n.s.

9 Feb. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entry: Dr Thomas — [Dewhurst]

Letter received: From John Hoskins (letter dated Dec. 10 [o.s.]) — [Journal 1677, ed. Lough]

  ▲  

Monday, 22 Feb. 1677, n.s.

12 Feb. 1677, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

Journal entries: Toilet, Empyema, Inflammatio, Gangrena, Tertian, Quartan, Epilepsie, Clavus — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Friday, 26 Feb. 1677, n.s.

16 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Montpellier to Zizian [Gigean], 3 leagues, from Zizian to Pezenas, 5 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 61]

Weather observations: “Rosemary bloom on Garigues as we passed. N.E. Misty, rain all day.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Journal entries: Pezenas, Grange, Spring — [Lough]

Saturday, 27 Feb. 1677, n.s.

17 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Pezenas to Beziers, 4 leagues ….” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 62]

Weather observations: “Close all day and some little rain. South.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; shorthand]

Sunday, 28 Feb. 1677, n.s.

18 Feb. 1677, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier, “[Wind] E. Rain and misty all day.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Journal entry: Beziers — [Lough]

Sources:

Dewhurst = John Locke (1632-1704), physician and philosopher : a medical biography : with an edition of the medical notes in his journals / by Kenneth Dewhurst. – London : Wellcome Historical Medical Library, 1963.
