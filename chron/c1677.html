1677

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Friday, 1 Jan. 1677, n.s.

22 Dec. 1676, o.s.

[At Montpellier].

February

Friday, 26 Feb. 1677, n.s.

16 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Montpellier to Zizian [Gigean], 3 leagues, from Zizian to Pezenas, 5 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 61]

Saturday, 27 Feb. 1677, n.s.

17 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Pezenas to Beziers, 4 leagues ….” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 62]

March

Monday, 1 March 1677, n.s.

19 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Beziers to Pisariay [Puisserguier], 2 leagues … From Pisariay to St. Chignan de la Corne [St. Chinian], 2 leagues … From St. Chignan to St. Ponx, 3 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 63]

Tuesday, 2 March 1677, n.s.

20 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From St. Ponx to St. Amand, 4 leagues … From St. Amand to Castres, 4 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 65-66]

Thursday, 4 March 1677, n.s.

22 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Castres to the Reservoir neare Revel [on the Canal du Languedoc], 4 leagues … From thence to Castle Naudary, 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 68]

Friday, 5 March 1677, n.s.

23 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Castlenaudari on the Canale’s side to the Basin, 2 leagues. … From the basin at Narouse back to Castlenaudary, 1 league.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 68-72]

Saturday, 6 March 1677, n.s.

24 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“To Carcasson, 6 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 72]

Monday, 8 March 1677, n.s.

26 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Carcasson to Narbone, 8 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 72]

Tuesday, 9 March 1677, n.s.

27 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Narbon to Beziers, 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 78]

Wednesday, 10 March 1677, n.s.

28 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Beziers to St. Ibery [St. Thibery], 3 leagues, from St. Obery to Loupian, 3 leagues, and from Loupian to Ballaruke baths, 1 league.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 78]

Thursday, 11 March 1677, n.s.

1 March 1677, o.s.

“From Balleruk to Port Sette [Sète], 1½ leagues. … From Sette to Frontignan 1 league. From Frontignan to Montpellier, 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 78-80]

Thursday, 25 March 1677, n.s.

15 March 1677, o.s.

“From Montpellier to Loupian 4 leagues. … From Loupian to St. Iberi 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 84]

Friday, 26 March 1677, n.s.

16 March 1677, o.s.

“From St. Iberi to Beziers 3 leagues. … From Beziers to Pusol [Puzols] 5 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 85, 89]

Saturday, 27 March 1677, n.s.

17 March 1677, o.s.

“From Puzol to Pecheuri [Puichéric] 3 leagues. … From Pecheuri to Carcassone, 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 89, 88 (pp. 84-89 repeated)]

Sunday, 28 March 1677, n.s.

18 March 1677, o.s.

“From Carcassone to Villepinte 4 leagues. … From Villepinte to Castelnaudari 2 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 88]

Monday, 29 March 1677, n.s.

19 March 1677, o.s.

“From Castelnaudary to Montesquieu by the Canal 5 leagues. … From Montesquieu to Tholose 5 leagues by the Canale.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 89]

Wednesday, 31 March 1677, n.s.

21 March 1677, o.s.

“From Tholouse to Verdune 5 leagues upon the Garonne.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 95]

April

Thursday, 1 April 1677, n.s.

22 March 1677, o.s.

“From Verdune to Avillar [Auvillar] 6 leagues. … From Avillar to Bouér [Boé] 4 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 96]

Friday, 2 April 1677, n.s.

23 March 1677, o.s.

“From Boüé to Agen half league.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 96]
“Frid Apr 2 I was taken ill at Agen …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 135]

Saturday, 3 April 1677, n.s.

24 March 1677, o.s.

“From Agen to Tonneins 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 97]

Sunday, 4 April 1677, n.s.

25 March 1677, o.s.

“From Tonneins to Cadillac 7 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 97]

Monday, 5 April 1677, n.s.

26 March 1677, o.s.

“From Cadillac to Bourdeaux 5 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 97]

May

Tuesday, 18 May 1677, n.s.

8 May 1677, o.s.

“From Bourdeaux to Blay 7 leagues by water.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 138]

Wednesday, 19 May 1677, n.s.

9 May 1677, o.s.

“From Blaye to Petit Niort 5½ leagues. … From thence to Pons 4½ leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 139]

Thursday, 20 May 1677, n.s.

10 May 1677, o.s.

“From Pons … to Escoyaux [Écoyeux] 6 leagues. … About the midle of the way we passed the Charrant, 2 leagues from Xantes [Saintes] …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 139-140]

Friday, 21 May 1677, n.s.

11 May 1677, o.s.

“From Escouyaux to Villedieu 6 leagues. … From Villedieu to St. Leger de Mel, 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 140]

Saturday, 22 May 1677, n.s.

12 May 1677, o.s.

“From St. Leger to Lusignan, 7 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 141]

Sunday, 23 May 1677, n.s.

13 May 1677, o.s.

“From Lusignan to Poictiers 5 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 141]

Tuesday, 25 May 1677, n.s.

15 May 1677, o.s.

“From Poictiers to St. Genai [St. Genest] 5 leagues. … From St. Gené to Richelieu 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 142]

Wednesday, 26 May 1677, n.s.

16 May 1677, o.s.

“From Richelieu to Pont Roan [Pont de Ruen] 7 leagues. … From Pont Roan to Tours 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 143-144]

Thursday, 27 May 1677, n.s.

17 May 1677, o.s.

“From Tours to Amboise 6 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 146]

Friday, 28 May 1677, n.s.

18 May 1677, o.s.

“From Amboise to Blois 10 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 147]

Saturday, 29 May 1677, n.s.

19 May 1677, o.s.

“From Blois to Clery 12 leagues. … From Clery to Orleance 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 147-148]

June

Tuesday, 1 June 1677, n.s.

22 May 1677, o.s.

“From Orleance to Toury 10 leagues. … From Toury to Estamps 10 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 149]

Wednesday, 2 June 1677, n.s.

23 May 1677, o.s.

“From Estampes to Chatres 6 leagues. From Chatres to Paris 8 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 149]

Wednesday, 23 June 1677, n.s.

13 May 1677, o.s.

“From Paris to Versales 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 169]

Thursday, 24 June 1677, n.s.

14 May 1677, o.s.

[Return to Paris]

July

August

Saturday, 14 August 1677, n.s.

4 August 1677, o.s.

[To St. Clou] — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 230]

Tuesday, 17 August 1677, n.s.

7 August 1677, o.s.

“From St. Clou to Meudon 1 league. … From Meudon to Isis [Issy] 1 league.” [Return to Paris?] — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 231-232]

Tuesday, 24 August 1677, n.s.

14 August 1677, o.s.

[To Versailles] — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 236]

Thursday, 26 August 1677, n.s.

16 August 1677, o.s.

[Return to Paris?] — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 244]

September

Thursday, 9 September 1677, n.s.

30 August 1677, o.s.

“From Paris to Chantilly 10 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 260]

Friday, 10 September 1677, n.s.

31 August 1677, o.s.

“From Chantilly to Liancour 4 leagues … From Liancour to Verneuile 2 leagues … From Verneuile to Chantilly 2 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 261-262]

Friday, 11 September 1677, n.s.

1 September 1677, o.s.

“From Chantilly to Champlastreux 4 leagues. … From Champlastreux to Equan[Écouen] 2 leagues … From Equam to Paris 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 262-263]

Thursday, 23 September 1677, n.s.

13 September 1677, o.s.

“From Paris to Ysole [Essonnes] 7 leagues, from Ysole to Fountaine bleau.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 273]

Monday, 27 September 1677, n.s.

17 September 1677, o.s.

“From Fountain bleau to Vaux [Vaux-le-Vicomte] 4 leagues.” [Returned to Paris before 20/30 September] — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 276, 279]

October

Wednesday, 6 October 1677, n.s.

26 September 1677, o.s.

“At Versailles.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 289]

Tuesday, 12 October 1677, n.s.

2 October 1677, o.s.

“At Versailles …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 289]

Tuesday, 26 October 1677, n.s.

16 October 1677, o.s.

“At Versailles …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 304]

November

Tuesday, 2 November 1677, n.s.

23 October 1677, o.s.

“At Ruell 2 leagues from Paris …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 316]
