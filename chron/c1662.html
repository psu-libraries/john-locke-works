1662

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Tuesday, 21 January 1662, o.s.

31 January 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 128]

March

Friday, 7 March 1662, o.s.

17 March 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Samuel Tilly; misdated [16]51, presumably 1661[/62]; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 129]

Tuesday, 18 March 1662, o.s.

28 March 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 130]

April

Sunday, 20 April 1662, o.s.

30 April 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke written: From John Strachey; dated only “Sunday night”; this date suggested by De Beer; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 131]

June

Monday, 30 June 1662, o.s.

10 July 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Robert Mandey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 133]

July

Friday, 4 July 1662, o.s.

14 July 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From George Percivall, Dublin; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 134]

Saturday, 12 July 1662, o.s.

22 July 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From George Percivall, Dublin; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 135]

Sunday, 27 July 1662, o.s.

6 August 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Lord Berkeley; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 136]

Letter dated: From Lord Berkeley to Sir Charles Berkeley. [De Beer, i. (137)]

Tuesday, 29 July 1662, o.s.

8 August 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Henry Townshend, Worcester; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 138]

Wednesday, 30 July 1662, o.s.

9 August 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Thomas Westrowe; the year is uncertain; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 139]

August

Sunday, 17 August 1662, o.s.

27 August 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke written: From Sir Charles Berkeley; undated; approximate date suggested by De Beer based on contents and postmark; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 140]

Tuesday, 19 August 1662, o.s.

29 August 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Henry Townshend, Worcester; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 141]

Letter to Locke dated: From Robert Mandey, Worcester; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 142]

Friday, 22 August 1662, o.s.

1 September 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Robert Mandey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 143]

Thursday, 28 August 1662, o.s.

7 September 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Andrew Crooke; postscript to Robert Mandey’s letter of 22 August. [De Beer, i. 143]

Friday, 29 August 1662, o.s.

8 September 1662, n.s.

Locke’s thirtieth birthday.

September

Friday, 19 September 1662, o.s.

29 September 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Robert Pickering, Thelwall; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 144]

Monday, 22 September 1662, o.s.

1 September 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Henry Townshend, Worcester; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 145]

Monday, 29 September 1662, o.s.

8 September 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Henry Barnard, Bridgenorth; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 146]

Date uncertain:

Work written (September? 1662): “Principi Daniae Oxonium ex Itinere divertenti 62”; Latin oration

October

Tuesday, 7 October 1662, o.s.

17 October 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From John Dod, Hinton; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 148]

Tuesday, 21 October 1662, o.s.

31 October 1662, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From [Mrs] Ellice Price. [De Beer, i. 149]

December

Wednesday, 31 December 1662, o.s.

10 January 1663, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 151]

Undated

Work written (late 1662?): Second Tract on Government. Internal evidence indicates it was completed by the end of January 1663; most likely written late in 1662. A surviving draft [MS. Locke e. 6, ff. 91-69 rev.] was written at an uncertain but earlier date.
