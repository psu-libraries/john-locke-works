John Locke Chronology

1662

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Tuesday, 21 January 1662, o.s.

31 January 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 128]

March

Friday, 7 March 1662, o.s.

17 March 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Samuel Tilly; misdated [16]51, presumably 1661[/62]; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 129]

Tuesday, 18 March 1662, o.s.

28 March 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 130]

April

Sunday, 20 April 1662, o.s.

30 April 1662, n.s.

Letter (undated): From John Strachey; dated only “Sunday night”; this date suggested by De Beer; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 131]

May

Wednesday, 21 May 1662, o.s.

31 May 1662, n.s.

Wedding of Charles II and Catharine of Braganza; commemorated by the Oxford anthology Domiduca Oxoniensis; which contained a poem by Locke. The collection may have been published before the wedding, but was definitely available by the end of June.

June

Monday, 30 June 1662, o.s.

10 July 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Robert Mandey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 133]

  ▲  

July

Friday, 4 July 1662, o.s.

14 July 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From George Percivall, Dublin; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 134]

Saturday, 12 July 1662, o.s.

22 July 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From George Percivall, Dublin; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 135]

Sunday, 27 July 1662, o.s.

6 August 1662, n.s.

Letters dated: From Lord Berkeley; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 136]; from Lord Berkeley to Sir Charles Berkeley. [De Beer, i. (137)]

Tuesday, 29 July 1662, o.s.

8 August 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Henry Townshend, Worcester; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 138]

Wednesday, 30 July 1662, o.s.

9 August 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Thomas Westrowe; the year is uncertain; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 139]

August

Sunday, 17 August 1662, o.s.

27 August 1662, n.s.

Letter (undated): From Sir Charles Berkeley; approximate date suggested by De Beer based on the contents and the postmark; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 140]

Tuesday, 19 August 1662, o.s.

29 August 1662, n.s.

Letters dated: From Henry Townshend, Worcester; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 141; from Robert Mandey, Worcester; addressed to Locke at Christ Church; includes a postscript from Andrew Crooke dated 28 August. [De Beer, i. 142]

Friday, 22 August 1662, o.s.

1 September 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Robert Mandey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 143]

Thursday, 28 August 1662, o.s.

7 September 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Andrew Crooke; postscript to Robert Mandeyrsquo;s letter of 22 August. [De Beer, i. 143]

Friday, 29 August 1662, o.s.

8 September 1662, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 30.

  ▲  

September

Friday, 19 September 1662, o.s.

29 September 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Robert Pickering, Thelwall; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 144]

Monday, 22 September 1662, o.s.

1 September 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Henry Townshend, Worcester; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 145]

Saturday, 27 September 1662, o.s.

6 October 1662, n.s.

Oration (September? 1662):   “Principi Daniae Oxonium ex Itinere divertenti 62”; Latin oration, delivered [?] 27 September, to welcome Prince Christian of Denmark to Christ Church, where he dined with the Dean.

Monday, 29 September 1662, o.s.

8 October 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From Henry Barnard, Bridgenorth; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 146]

October

Tuesday, 7 October 1662, o.s.

17 October 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Dod, Hinton; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 148]

Tuesday, 21 October 1662, o.s.

31 October 1662, n.s.

Letter dated: From [Mrs] Ellice Price. [De Beer, i. 149]

December

Wednesday, 31 December 1662, o.s.

10 January 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 151]

Undated

Work written (late 1662?):   Second Tract on Government. Internal evidence indicates it was completed by the end of January 1663; most likely written late in 1662. A surviving draft [MS. Locke e. 6, ff. 91-69 rev.] was written at an uncertain but earlier date.

  ▲  

Sources:
De Beer = The correspondence of John Locke / edited by E. S. de Beer. – Oxford : Clarendon Press, 1976-89. – 8 vols. – (Clarendon edition of the works of John Locke)
