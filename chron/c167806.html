Wednesday, 1 June 1678, n.s.

22 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Digestion, Cornelio, Glas drops, Montanari, Spanemio, Numismata, Ciocolatte, Pimienta chapa, Salivatio, Nati, Botanica — [Lough “Reading”, Dewhurst].

Letter: From Robert Huntington, dated 22 May 1678 o.s.; written from Aleppo; addressed to Locke in London; answers “your Letter to of March the 3d at Paris”. [Corr., i. 382]

Person encountered: Robert Huntington (letter)

Thursday, 2 June 1678, n.s.

23 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Quartana, Dysenteria, Hepaticum, Gabriel de Philadelph, Dandini — [Dewhurst, Lough “Reading”].

Friday, 3 June 1678, n.s.

24 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Quartana, Salivatio — [Dewhurst].

Saturday, 4 June 1678, n.s.

25 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Stringer, Gravier — [Lough].

  ▲  

Sunday, 5 June 1678, n.s.

26 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Invalids — [Lough].

Monday, 6 June 1678, n.s.

27 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Salivatio — [Dewhurst].

Tuesday, 7 June 1678, n.s.

28 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough].

Thursday, 9 June 1678, n.s.

30 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Pontgibault, Spring — [Lough].

Saturday, 11 June 1678, n.s.

1 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Revenue — [Lough].

Sunday, 12 June 1678, n.s.

2 June 1678, o.s.

Letter: From Dr. Thomas Coxe, dated 2 June 1678 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke in Paris; answered by Locke on 13/23 June 1678. [Corr., i. 383]

Person encountered: Dr. Thomas Coxe (letter)

  ▲  

Monday, 13 June 1678, n.s.

3 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Tendo sectus — [Dewhurst].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 3 June 1678 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; answers ”yours of the 28 May”. [Corr., i. 384]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Tuesday, 14 June 1678, n.s.

4 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: From William Charleton, dated 14 June 1678 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 385]

Person encountered: William Charleton (letter)

Wednesday, 15 June 1678, n.s.

5 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Salivatio — [Dewhurst].

Saturday, 18 June 1678, n.s.

8 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Osby, Liturges, Gabriel de Philadelph, Eucarist — [Lough, Lough “Reading”].

  ▲  

Monday, 20 June 1678, n.s.

10 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Podagra, Calculus, Quartan, Febris, Dysentery — [Lough].

Wednesday, 22 June 1678, n.s.

12 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Arthritis [twice], Epilepsia [twice], Aurum potabile, Quartan, Williams, Hydrops [three times]   [Dewhurst].

Letter: To Sir John Banks, dated 22 June 1678 n.s. (lost); answered by Banks on 20/30 June 1678. [see Corr., i. 387]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Thursday, 23 June 1678, n.s.

13 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Gun, Bonfires   [Lough].

Letter: To Dr. Thomas Coxe, dated 23 June 1678 n.s. (draft); written in Paris; answers Dr. Coxe’s letter of 2[/12] June 1677. [Corr., i. 386]

Person encountered: Dr. Thomas Coxe (letter)

Friday, 24 June 1678, n.s.

14 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Brignols, Exchange, Hostel de Guise   [Lough].

Letter: To Thomas Stringer, written 35 June 1678 n.s. (lost); answered by Stringer on 3/13 July 1678. [see Corr., i. 387]

Person encountered: Thomas Stringer (letter)

  ▲  

Sunday, 26 June 1678, n.s.

16 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Hostel de Ferte   [Lough].

Tuesday, 28 June 1678, n.s.

18 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Cascade, Lake of Eyre, Water, Aquae minerales, Gonorrhea venereal, Fluor albus, Diluteing, Soule, Clos   [Lough, Dewhurst].

Thursday, 30 June 1678, n.s.

20 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: C.B. no. 1, C.B. no. 2, C.B. no. 3, C.B. no. 4, C.B. no. 5   [Lough “Reading”].

As Locke and his pupil Caleb Banks prepare to leave Paris in early July, Locke packs up his books into five boxes to be left in Paris, carefully listing the contents of each box.

Letter: From Sir John Banks, daed 20 June 1678 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; answers “yours of the 22 instant”. [Corr., i. 387]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)
