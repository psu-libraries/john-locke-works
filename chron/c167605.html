Friday, 1 May 1676, n.s.

21 April 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “[Wind] E. Fair. Warme, but snow lyeing on the hils beyond Peire St. Loup.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Reditus, Tributa, Protestantes, Puerperum — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Saturday, 2 May 1676, n.s.

22 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Cerae albifactio, Cera dealbanda, Jasmini ol: — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Wednesday, 6 May 1676, n.s.

26 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Lignum siccandum, Roupie — [Lough]

Thursday, 7 May 1676, n.s.

27 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Vinea, Flos, Dolor, Calor, Miracula, Austeritas, Lignum denigrandum — [Lough]

Friday, 8 May 1676, n.s.

28 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Stuprum, Fornie, Adulter, Papa, Metempsychosis, Roupie

Saturday, 9 May 1676, n.s.

29 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entries: Aurantia, Balaruc waters — [Lough]

  ▲  

Sunday, 10 May 1676, n.s.

30 April 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier.

Journal entry: Denier — [Lough]

“Sent by Mr. Nevock to Mr. Stringer some seeds of Sycamore of Malta & Sabina baccifera. £10 in deniers weighes 12 lb. 10 ozs.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]; Stringer confirmed receipt in his letter of 5/15 June 1676 – [De Beer, i. 312]

Journal entries: Moneta Hispan, Moneta Avign, Pasta
