1655

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

February

Tuesday, 6 February 1654/5, o.s.

Note: Early medical notes. A note on page 144 is dated “Feb. 6, 1654/5.” [MS Locke e. 4]

July

Date uncertain

Letter: “G.R.” [Thomas Grenfeild?], [July 1655?]; probably answered by Grenfeild on 2 August. [De Beer, i. 18]

August

Thursday, 2 August 1655, o.s.

12 August 1655, n.s.

Letter: From Thomas Grenfeild, dated 2 August 1655; written at Pensford. probably an answer to Locke’s letter of [July?]; probably answered by Locke in mid-August. [De Beer, i. 19]

Saturday, 11 August 1655, o.s.

21 August 1655, n.s.

Letter: “To my Cosin Martine”, dated 11 August 1655; written at Oxford. [De Beer, i. 21]

Sunday, 29 August 1655, o.s.

8 September 1655, n.s.

Locke’s birthday; age 23.

Date uncertain

Letter: “T.G.V.” [Thomas Grenfeild?], [mid-August 1655?]; probably an answer to Grenfeild’s letter of 2 August. [De Beer, i. 20]

September

Tuesday, 11 September 1655, o.s.

21 September 1655, n.s.

Letter: From Samuel Tilly, dated 11 September 1655 at Oxford; addressed to Locke at Pensford. Answers Locke’s letter of [early September?]; answered by Locke in [late September?]. [De Beer, i. 23]

Date uncertain

  • To [Samuel Tilly?], [early September 1655?]; written by Locke at Pensford to Tilly at Oxford. Answered by Tilly on 11 September. [De Beer, i. 22]
  • To [Samuel Tilly?], [late September 1655?]; answers Tilly’s letter of 11 September. [De Beer, i. 24]
