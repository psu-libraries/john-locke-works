Tuesday, 8 November 1678, n.s.

29 October 1678, o.s.

At Lyon.

Letters dated: To Dr. John Mapletoft [De Beer, i. 417]; to Nicolas Toinard [De Beer, i. 418]; both written at Lyon.

“ If all the world should goe to Rome I thinke I should never, haveing been twice firmely bent upon it, the time set, the company agreed, and so many times defeated. I came hither in all hast from Montpellier … with the same designe: but old father Winter, armed with all his snow and isecles, keeps gard on Montsenny [Mont Cenis], and will not let me passe.” — [Locke to Mapletoft, De Beer, i. 417]
