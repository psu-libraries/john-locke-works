Saturday, 2 July 1678, n.s.

22 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Castalio, Bosius — [Lough].

Monday, 4 July 1678, n.s.

24 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Rabets, Dalbon, Bible, Claude, Prejeggez paior, Innocent III — [Lough, Lough “Reading”].

Tuesday, 5 July 1678, n.s.

25 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Papa, Glas drops, Rosetti, Montenari, Marchetti, Pascal — [Lough “Reading”].

Wednesday, 6 July 1678, n.s.

26 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Stringer, Daulphin — [Lough].

Thursday, 7 July 1678, n.s.

27 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Herinx — [Lough].

Friday, 8 July 1678, n.s.

28 June 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Brisban, B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Stringer, C.B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Exchange — [Lough].

Saturday, 9 July 1678, n.s.

29 June 1678, o.s.

“From Paris to Longemeaux [Longjumeau] 4 Leagues, about a mile from which on the top of the hill is the Chasteau de Chayly [Chilly] …” [From thence to Tours] — [Journal 1678, page 190]

Journal entries: B. [i.e. Caleb Banks], Longemeaux, Chayly — [Lough].

Letters dated: To Nicolas Toinard [in French]. — [De Beer, i. 388]; to Sir John Banks (no longer survives). — [see De Beer, i. 391]

  ▲  

Monday, 11 July 1678, n.s.

1 July 1678, o.s.

“From Tours to Orleans 10 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 191]

Journal entries: Tours, Orleans, Virgin, Plutarch — [Lough].

Tuesday, 12 July 1678, n.s.

2 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Glasse house, Pucelle, Lame & crooked, Tussis, Arthritis, Bruta sentiunt, Tradition — [Lough, Dewhurst].

Wednesday, 13 July 1678, n.s.

3 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Orleans, Townhouse, Trochisques, Hand mill, Celeste phagadenica, MM [i.e. Materia medica] — [Lough, Dewhurst].

Letter dated: From Thomas Stringer; addressed to Locke in Paris; received (?) 11/21 July. — [De Beer, i. 389]

Thursday, 14 July 1678, n.s.

4 July 1678, o.s.

“We were at the source of the Loiret … From hence to Olivet which [is] about 1/3 the way to Orleans.” [Return to Orléans] — [Journal 1678, page 196]

Journal entries: Loiret, Olivet — [Lough].

Letter dated: To Nicolas Toinard, Paris [in French]; written at Orléans. — [De Beer, i. 388]

Friday, 15 July 1678, n.s.

5 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Hysterica, Pleuritis, Colica, Atlantis, Lex, Lex na[tur]ae — [Dewhurst, Bellatella, Goldie].

Saturday, 16 July 1678, n.s.

6 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Epilepsia, Convulsio, Vertigo, Stomachus, Vomitorium, Quartan [in shorthand], Puneses, Renum calor, Infinitum — [Dewhurst, Lough, Aaron & Gibb].

  ▲  

Sunday, 17 July 1678, n.s.

7 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Crocus, Alkahest — [Dewhurst].

Monday, 18 July 1678, n.s.

8 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Cholera sicca, Diarrhaea, Pestis, Fluor albus [in Latin], Intermittentes [in Latin], Opium [in Latin] — [Dewhurst].

Tuesday, 19 July 1678, n.s.

9 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Deauratio [in Latin], Magnetisme [in Latin], Vertigo, Haemorrhoids — [Dewhurst].

Wednesday, 20 July 1678, n.s.

10 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Hysterica [in Latin], Hemina, Septalius, Cotyla, Congium, Alpinus, Pestis [in Latin], Deliquium [in Latin], Capitis dolor [in Latin], Epilepsia [in Latin; twice], Apoplexia [in Latin] — [Dewhurst].

Thursday, 21 July 1678, n.s.

11 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Stringer, Taxes, Burthens — [Lough].

Letters dated: To Nicolas Toinard, Paris [in Latin]; written at Orléans. — [De Beer, i. 390A]; from Sir John Banks, Lincoln’s Inn Fields, London. — [De Beer, i. 391]

Letter received (?): From Thomas Stringer; sent 3/13 July. — [De Beer, i. 389]

Friday, 22 July 1678, n.s.

12 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Joubertus [in Latin], Mercurius [in Latin], Partus [in Latin], MM [i.e. Materia medica; in Latin], Vertigo — [Dewhurst].

Letters dated: From Nicolas Toinard, Paris; addressed to Locke at Orléans. — [De Beer, i. 392]

Saturday, 23 July 1678, n.s.

13 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Mill, Hand mill, Amphora, Congius, Sextarius, Cyothus.

  ▲  

Sunday, 24 July 1678, n.s.

14 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Letters dated: From Nicolas Toinard, Paris; addressed to Locke at Orléans. — [De Beer, i. 393]

Monday, 25 July 1678, n.s.

15 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entry: Gangraena [in shorthand] — [Dewhurst].

Tuesday, 26 July 1678, n.s.

16 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Letters dated: To Nicolas Toinard, Paris [in Latin]; written at Orléans. — [De Beer, i. 394]

Thursday, 28 July 1678, n.s.

18 July 1678, o.s.

At Orléans.

Journal entries: Venerea [in Latin], Catharticum [in Latin], Oris ulcera [in Latin], Charcoals, Gardening, Dolores, Contusio [in Latin], Cardiacum [in Latin], Clavus pedis [in Latin], Intermittentes [in Latin], Phthisis [in Latin], Acquirenda, Salvia, Caprifolium semper virens, Ocymum, Jalap, Scabies [in Latin], Asthma [in Latin], Godfroy — [Dewhurst, Lough].

Saturday, 30 July 1678, n.s.

20 July 1678, o.s.

“From Orleans to Avery [Avary] 8 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 227]

Journal entries: Avery, Pears, Plumbs — [Lough].

Sunday, 31 July 1678, n.s.

21 July 1678, o.s.

“From Avery to Bloys [Blois] 7 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 229]

Journal entries: Bloys, Menars — [Lough].
