Saturday, 9 July 1678, n.s.

29 June 1678, o.s.

“From Paris to Longemeaux [Longjumeau] 4 Leagues, about a mile from which on the top of the hill is the Chasteau de Chayly [Chilly] …” [From thence to Toury] — [Journal 1678, page 190]

Monday, 11 July 1678, n.s.

1 July 1678, o.s.

“From Toury to Orleans 10 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 191]

Saturday, 30 July 1678, n.s.

20 July 1678, o.s.

“From Orleans to Avery 8 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 227]

Sunday, 31 July 1678, n.s.

21 July 1678, o.s.

“From Avery to Bloys 7 leagues.” — [Journal 1678, page 229]
