January

Wednesday, 1 Jan. 1676, n.s.

22 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Avignon

Journal entries: Nostre Dame, St. Peter’s, Judaei, Jews, Avignon, Rodanus, Rhosne — [Lough]

Thursday, 2 Jan. 1676, n.s.

23 Dec. 1675, o.s.

[At Avignon]. “Hence we went to Pont du Gard, 4 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 27]

Journal entries: Pont du Gard, Vineae — [Lough]

Friday, 3 Jan. 1676, n.s.

24 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We went from Pont du Gard to Nismes, 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 28]

Journal entries: Nismes, Amhitheaer, Deyron, Pont du Gard, Protestantes, La Religion, Nismes, Bombicina — [Lough]

Saturday, 4 Jan. 1676, n.s.

25 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“We arrived at Montpellier late in the night, having dined at a Protestant Inn at Lunel, 3 leagues from Montpellier.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 35]

Journal entry: Montpellier — [Lough]

Sunday, 5 Jan. 1676, n.s.

26 Dec. 1675, o.s.

“Having horses, we rode out to the seaside, which is a league from the town southward.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 35]

Monday, 6 Jan. 1676, n.s.

27 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Vectura, Portage, Dinner, Lodging, Horsemeat — [Lough]

Tuesday, 7 Jan. 1676, n.s.

28 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entry: Pension — [Lough]

Wednesday, 8 Jan. 1676, n.s.

29 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entry: Olives — [Lough]

Thursday, 9 Jan. 1676, n.s.

30 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entry: Olivae — [Lough]

Friday, 10 Jan. 1676, n.s.

31 Dec. 1675, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Walks, Hortus — [Lough]

February

March

Wednesday, 4 March 1676, n.s.

23 Feb. 1676, o.s.

“We went to Frontignan, 4 leagues. … From hence we went to Port Cette [Sète]. … Cette is 1 ½ leagues from Frontignan. … From hence we went to Balaruc [Balaruc-les-Bains] & lay there.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 138-140]

Thurssday, 5 March 1676, n.s.

24 Feb. 1676, o.s.

[Returned to Montpellier ?]

Tuesday, 24 March 1676, n.s.

14 March 1676, o.s.

“Dined at Pont Lunell, 4 Leagues from Montpellier. … From thence to Aigues Mortes, 2 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 161]

Wednesday, 25 March 1676, n.s.

15 March 1676, o.s.

“We went to Pecais, 2 leagues. … We lay at night at Pont Lunell.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 164, 168]

Thursday, 26 March 1676, n.s.

16 March 1676, o.s.

“From Pont Lunel to Castret [Castries], 2 long leagues.” [Returned to Montpellier ?] — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 169]

April

Monday, 6 April 1676, n.s.

27 March 1676, o.s.

“To Pont Lunell to diner, 4 leagues. From thence over the valy of Aigues Mortes, 2 leagues … At Bauvair [Vauvert] … To St. Giles 2 leagues more …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 180-181]

Tuesday, 7 April 1676, n.s.

28 March 1676, o.s.

“From St. Giles to Arles 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 182]

Wednesday, 8 April 1676, n.s.

29 March 1676, o.s.

“From Arles to St. Martins to dinner. 3 leagues to St. Martin de Crau. … From St. Martin to Salon to bed, 4 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 189-190]

Thursday, 9 April 1676, n.s.

30 March 1676, o.s.

“From Sallon to the Griffon, 5 leagues … From the Griffon to Marseilles 3½ leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 194]

Sunday, 12 April 1676, n.s.

2 April 1676, o.s.

“We left Marseilles … and lay that night at Maison brulée [Le Brûlat], a single house near Castilet, 6 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 198]

Monday, 13 April 1676, n.s.

3 April 1676, o.s.

“From Maison brullé to Toulon, 3½ leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 200]

Tuesday, 14 April 1676, n.s.

4 April 1676, o.s.

“From Toulon to Hyeres, 3 leagues to bed.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 204]

Wednesday, 15 April 1676, n.s.

5 April 1676, o.s.

“From Hyeres to Maun [Méounes] to dinner, 4 leagues … 2 leagues from Hyeres we came to Solier [Solliès] … From thence to Beaugency [Belgentier?], 1 league … From Meaunes to St. Baum, 3 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 206, 209]

Thursday, 16 April 1676, n.s.

6 April 1676, o.s.

“From St. Baume to St. Maximin, 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 210]

Friday, 17 April 1676, n.s.

7 April 1676, o.s.

“From St. Maximin to Aix, 6 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 210]

Monday, 20 April 1676, n.s.

10 April 1676, o.s.

“From Aix to Lambesc, 3 leagues … From Lambesc to Cavaillon, 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 218]

Tuesday, 21 April 1676, n.s.

11 April 1676, o.s.

“From Cavaillon to Vaucluse, 2 leagues … From Vaucluse to Lisle [L’Isle-sur-la-Sorgue], 1 league, from Lisle to Avignon, 4 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 219-220]

Wednesday, 22 April 1676, n.s.

12 April 1676, o.s.

“At Ville Neuf over against Avignon on the other side [of] the Rhosne … From Avignon to Tarascon, 4 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, pp. 221-222]

Thursday, 23 April 1676, n.s.

13 April 1676, o.s.

“From Tarascon to Generat [Générac], 4 leagues, & from Generat to Pont Lunel 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 226]

Friday, 24 April 1676, n.s.

14 April 1676, o.s.

“From Pont Lunel to Montpellier, 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 229]

15 June 1676, o.s.

“Went to Selneuf [Celleneuve] to Mr. Michard’s.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 302]
