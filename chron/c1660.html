John Locke Chronology

1660

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Monday, 9 January 1660, o.s.

19 January 1660, n.s.

Letter: To John Locke, sen., [ca. 9 January 1660]; addressed to John Locke, sen., at Pensford; the date is approximate, based on the contents of the letter. [De Beer, i. 91]

Person encountered: John Locke, sen. (letter).

February

Friday, 3 February 1660, o.s.

13 February 1660, n.s.

Letter: From William Carr, [3 February 1660]; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford; the date is approximate, based on the contents of the letter. Answered by Locke on 5 March. [De Beer, i. 92]

Person encountered: William Carr (letter)

March

Monday, 5 March 1660, o.s.

15 March 1660, n.s.

Letter: To William Carr, dated 5 March [1660]; not a draft, but probably not sent; addressed to Carr “at Colonel Pophams at Lincolne howse Westminster”. answer to Carr’s letter of 3 February. [De Beer, i. 93]

Person encountered: William Carr (letter); Alexander Popham (letter)

Thursday, 15 March 1660, o.s.

25 March 1660, n.s.

Letter: To William Carr (draft), dated 15 March [1660]; written at Christ Church Oxford; addressed to Carr at Lincoln House, Westiminster. The year is supplied by De Beer, based on the contents of the letter. [De Beer, i. 94]

Person encountered: William Carr (letter)

  ▲  

April

Tuesday, 10 April 1660, o.s.

20 April 1660, n.s.

Letter: To John Locke, sen., dated 10 April [1660]; the year is based on the contents of the letter. [De Beer, i. 95]

Person encountered: John Locke, sen. (letter).

Date Uncertain

Letter: To P A [Alexander Popham] (draft), [ca. April 1660?]; approximate date based on the contents of the letter. [De Beer, i. 96]

Person encountered: P A [Alexander Popham] (letter).

May

Sunday, 20 May 1660, o.s.

30 May 1660, n.s.

Letter: From Dr. Ayliffe Ivye, dated 20 May 1660; written at Brislington; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. The letter contains the first mention of Robert Boyle in Locke’s correspondence.

[De Beer, i. 97]

Persons encountered: Dr. Ayliffe Ivye (letter); Robert Boyle (letter).

Thursday, 24 May 1660, o.s.

3 June 1660, n.s.

Letter: From John Strachey to John Locke, sen., dated 24 May 1660; addressed to Locke sen. at Pensford. [De Beer, i. (98)]

Persons encountered: John Strachey (letter); John Locke, sen. (letter).

June

Saturday, 30 June 1660, o.s.

10 July 1660, n.s.

Letter: From Thomas Westrowe; dated “July 30th 1660”, which is incorrect; De Beer suggests June instead. Written in London. [De Beer, i. 99]

Person encountered: Thomas Westrowe (letter).

  ▲  

July

Saturday, 7 July 1660, o.s.

17 July 1660, n.s.

Publication: Britannia rediviva (purchased by Thomason on 7 July); contained a poem by Locke.

Thursday, 19 July 1660, o.s.

29 July 1660, n.s.

Letter: From [Thomas Westrowe?], dated 19 July 1660. [De Beer, i. 100]

Person encountered: Thomas Westrowe (letter).

Monday, 30 July 1660, o.s.

9 August 1660, n.s.

Letter: From Thomas Westrowe dated 30 July 1660; the date is incorrect; De Beer suggests June 30 instead. [De Beer, i. 99]

August

Wednesday, 29 August 1660, o.s.

8 September 1660, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 28.

Letter dated: From George Percivall; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 102]

September

Saturday, 22 September 1660, o.s.

2 October 1660, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey; the year is uncertain. [De Beer, i. 103]

  ▲  

October

Tuesday, 23 October 1660, o.s.

2 November 1660, n.s.

Letter dated: From Gabriel Towerson; addressed to Locke at Pensford. [De Beer, i. 104]

Tuesday, 30 October 1660, o.s.

9 November 1660, n.s.

Letter (undated): From William Uvedale; addressed to Locke at Pensford; approximate date suggested by De Beer. [De Beer, i. 105]

November

Saturday, 3 November 1660, o.s.

13 November 1660, n.s.

Letter (undated): From Gabriel Towerson; addressed to Locke at Pensford; approximate date suggested by De Beer. [De Beer, i. 106]

Thursday, 22 November 1660, o.s.

2 December 1660, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Oxford. [De Beer, i. 107]

December

Tuesday, 11 December 1660, o.s.

21 December 1660, n.s.

Letter dated: To [Gabriel Towerson?]; draft letter accompanying Locke’s First Tract on Government. [De Beer, i. 108]

Wednesday, 19 December 1660, o.s.

29 December 1660, n.s.

Letter dated: From George Percivall; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 109]

Thursday, 20 December 1660, o.s.

30 December 1660, n.s.

Letter dated: To John Locke, sen.; the year is supplied, based on the date of the recipient’s death (7 February 1661). [De Beer, i. 110]

Date Uncertain

Work written (September-December 1660):   First tract on government.

  ▲  

Sources:
De Beer = The correspondence of John Locke / edited by E. S. de Beer. – Oxford : Clarendon Press, 1976-89. – 8 vols. – (Clarendon edition of the works of John Locke)
