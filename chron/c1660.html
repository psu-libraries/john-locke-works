John Locke Chronology

[ Table of Contents ]

1660

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Monday, 9 January 1660, o.s.

19 January 1660, n.s.

Letter written: To John Locke, sen.; the date is approximate, based on the contents of the letter. [De Beer, i. 91]

February

Friday, 3 February 1660, o.s.

13 February 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke written: From William Carr; undated, date based on contents. [De Beer, i. 92]

March

Monday, 5 March 1660, o.s.

15 March 1660, n.s.

Letter written (but probably never sent): To William Carr; reply to Carrös letter of 3 February. [De Beer, i. 93]

Thursday, 15 March 1660, o.s.

25 March 1660, n.s.

[At Oxford].

Letter written: To William Carr; the year is supplied by De Beer, based on the contents. [De Beer, i. 94]

April

Tuesday, 10 April 1660, o.s.

20 April 1660, n.s.

Letter written: To John Locke, sen.; the year supplied by De Beer, based on the contents. [De Beer, i. 95]

May

Sunday, 20 May 1660, o.s.

30 May 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Dr Ayliffe Ivye; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 97]

Thursday, 24 May 1660, o.s.

3 June 1660, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey to John Locke, sen. [De Beer, i. (98)]

June

Saturday, 30 June 1660, o.s.

10 July 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke written: From Thomas Westrowe; dated “July 30th 1660”, which is incorrect; De Beer suggests June instead. [De Beer, i. 99]

July

Saturday, 19 July 1660, o.s.

19 Thursday 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From [Thomas Westrowe?]. [De Beer, i. 100]

Monday, 30 July 1660, o.s.

9 August 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Thomas Westrowe; the date is incorrect; De Beer suggests June 30 instead. [De Beer, i. 99]

August

Wednesday, 29 August 1660, o.s.

8 September 1660, n.s.

Locke’s twenty-eighth birthday.

Letter to Locke dated: From George Percivall; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 102]

September

Saturday, 22 September 1660, o.s.

2 October 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From John Strachey; the year is uncertain. [De Beer, i. 103]

October

Tuesday, 23 October 1660, o.s.

2 November 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From Gabriel Towerson; addressed to Locke at Pensford. [De Beer, i. 104]

Tuesday, 30 October 1660, o.s.

9 November 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke written: From William Uvedale; undated letter addressed to Locke at Pensford; approximate date suggested by De Beer. [De Beer, i. 105]

November

Saturday, 3 November 1660, o.s.

13 November 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke written: From Gabriel Towerson; undated letter addressed to Locke at Pensford; approximate date suggested by De Beer. [De Beer, i. 106]

Thursday, 22 November 1660, o.s.

2 December 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From John Strachey; addressed to Locke at Oxford. [De Beer, i. 107]

December

Tuesday, 11 December 1660, o.s.

21 December 1660, n.s.

Letter written: To [Gabriel Towerson?]; draft letter accompanying Locke’s First Tract on Government. [De Beer, i. 108]

Wednesday, 19 December 1660, o.s.

29 December 1660, n.s.

Letter to Locke dated: From George Percivall; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 109]

Thursday, 20 December 1660, o.s.

30 December 1660, n.s.

Letter written: To John Locke, sen.; the year is supplied, based on the date of the recipient’s death (7 February 1661). [De Beer, i. 110]

Undated

Work written (September-December 1660):   First tract on government.
