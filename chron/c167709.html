Thursday, 9 September 1677, n.s.

30 August 1677, o.s.

“From Paris to Chantilly 10 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 260]

Friday, 10 September 1677, n.s.

31 August 1677, o.s.

“From Chantilly to Liancour 4 leagues … From Liancour to Verneuile 2 leagues … From Verneuile to Chantilly 2 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 261-262]

Friday, 11 September 1677, n.s.

1 September 1677, o.s.

“From Chantilly to Champlastreux 4 leagues. … From Champlastreux to Equan[Écouen] 2 leagues … From Equam to Paris 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 262-263]

Thursday, 23 September 1677, n.s.

13 September 1677, o.s.

“From Paris to Ysole [Essonnes] 7 leagues, from Ysole to Fountaine bleau.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 273]

Monday, 27 September 1677, n.s.

17 September 1677, o.s.

“From Fountain bleau to Vaux [Vaux-le-Vicomte] 4 leagues.” [Returned to Paris before 20/30 September] — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 276, 279]
