September

Tuesday, 1 September 1676, n.s.

22 August 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Celleneuve: “Scarce a cloud, the hottest day of the year hitherto. The last 7 or 8 days the hottest we had all summer.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; shorthand]

Journal entry: Knowledge — [Lough, Von Leyden]

Monday, 7 September 1676, n.s.

28 August 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entry: Venerea — [Dewhurst]

Tuesday, 8 September 1676, n.s.

29 August 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Locke’s birthday, age 44

Wednesday, 9 September 1676, n.s.

30 August 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Celleneuve: “[Wind] N.E., fair, cold. From hence I saw to-day the Pyreneans, but dimly. The other day I saw them as plain as Pierre St. Loup. They lie S.W. and are, as they say, 50 leagues off.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Thursday, 10 September 1676, n.s.

31 August 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Celleneuve: “Cold, fire comfortable.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Friday, 11 September 1676, n.s.

1 September 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entry: Empyema — [Dewhurst]

Saturday, 12 September 1676, n.s.

2 September 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “[M]isty, rain, thunder & lightening, and hard rain at night.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

  ▲  

Monday, 14 September 1676, n.s.

4 September 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve

Journal entry: Sports — [Dewhurst]

Letter dated: From Joseph West. — [De Beer, i. 317]

Tuesday, 15 September 1676, n.s.

5 September 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “Close. Thunder, lightening & hard rain.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Friday, 18 September 1676, n.s.

8 September 1676, o.s.

[Returned to Montpellier]. — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 1, p. 442]

Saturday, 19 September 1676, n.s.

9 September 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entry: Species — [Aaron & Gibb]

  ▲  

Tuesday, 22 September 1676, n.s.

12 September 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “Fair, cold.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; shorthand]

Journal entry: Grape — [Aaron & Gibb]

Wednesday, 23 September 1676, n.s.

13 September 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Letter dated: From Mrs. Anna Grigg. — [De Beer, i. 319]

Saturday, 26 September 1676, n.s.

16 September 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Montpellier: “Thunder, lightening & raine.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Happynesse, Thomas — [Goldie, Lough]

Tuesday, 29 September 1676, n.s.

19 September 1676, o.s.

At Montpellier

Journal entries: Revenue [repeated on pages 447-448], Salt, Revenue of the Church, Church of France — [Lough]
