Tarascon

1676 Apr 12/22: Locke traveled from Avignon to Villeneuve, and on to Tarascon.

Thanet House, London

1677 Aug 16/26: Letter to Locke from Thomas Stringer at Thanet House.

Thelwall

1662 Sep 19: Letter to Locke from Robert Pickering at Thelwall.

Tillart

1675 Nov 23/Dec 3: Locke traveled from Poix to Beauvais and on to Tillart.

Toulon

1676 Apr 3/13: Locke traveled from Le Brûlat to Toulon.

Toulouse

1677 Mar 19/29-21/31?: Letter from Stringer to Locke at Toulouse (lost).

1677 Apr 4/14: Letter from Jacques Selapris to Locke at Toulouse.

Tournus

1675 Dec 9/19: Locke traveled by boat from Châlons-sur-Saône to Tournus and on to Mascon.

Trévoux

1675 Dec 10/20: Locke traveled by boat from Mascon to Belville and on to Ville Franche, Trévoux and Lyon.

Trinity College Cambridge

1663 Jan 15?: Letter to Locke from Sir Charles Berkeley at Trinity.
