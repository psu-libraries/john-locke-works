Générac

1676 Apr 13/23: Locke traveled from Tarascon to Générac, and on to Pont Lunel.

Gravesend

1675 Nov 12: Locke went to Gravesend en route to France.

Gray’s Inn, London

1656 Dec 10: Locke admitted to Gray’s Inn.

Gresham College, London

1676 Jun 28/Jul 8: Letter to Locke from Dr. John Mapletoft at Gresham College.

The Griffon

1676 Mar 30/Apr 9: Locke traveled from Salon to the Griffon, and on to Marseilles.
