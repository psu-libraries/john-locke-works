Monday, 1 March 1677, n.s.

19 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Beziers to Pisariay [Puisserguier], 2 leagues … From Pisariay to St. Chignan de la Corne [St. Chinian], 2 leagues … From St. Chignan to St. Ponx, 3 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 63]

Tuesday, 2 March 1677, n.s.

20 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From St. Ponx to St. Amand, 4 leagues … From St. Amand to Castres, 4 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 65-66]

Thursday, 4 March 1677, n.s.

22 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Castres to the Reservoir neare Revel [on the Canal du Languedoc], 4 leagues … From thence to Castle Naudary, 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 68]

Friday, 5 March 1677, n.s.

23 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Castlenaudari on the Canale’s side to the Basin, 2 leagues. … From the basin at Narouse back to Castlenaudary, 1 league.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 68-72]

Saturday, 6 March 1677, n.s.

24 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“To Carcasson, 6 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 72]

Monday, 8 March 1677, n.s.

26 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Carcasson to Narbone, 8 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 72]

Tuesday, 9 March 1677, n.s.

27 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Narbon to Beziers, 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 78]

Wednesday, 10 March 1677, n.s.

28 Feb. 1677, o.s.

“From Beziers to St. Ibery [St. Thibery], 3 leagues, from St. Obery to Loupian, 3 leagues, and from Loupian to Ballaruke baths, 1 league.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 78]

Thursday, 11 March 1677, n.s.

1 March 1677, o.s.

“From Balleruk to Port Sette [Sète], 1½ leagues. … From Sette to Frontignan 1 league. From Frontignan to Montpellier, 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 78-80]

Thursday, 25 March 1677, n.s.

15 March 1677, o.s.

“From Montpellier to Loupian 4 leagues. … From Loupian to St. Iberi 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 84]

Friday, 26 March 1677, n.s.

16 March 1677, o.s.

“From St. Iberi to Beziers 3 leagues. … From Beziers to Pusol [Puzols] 5 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 85, 89]

Saturday, 27 March 1677, n.s.

17 March 1677, o.s.

“From Puzol to Pecheuri [Puichéric] 3 leagues. … From Pecheuri to Carcassone, 3 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, pp. 89, 88 (pp. 84-89 repeated)]

Sunday, 28 March 1677, n.s.

18 March 1677, o.s.

“From Carcassone to Villepinte 4 leagues. … From Villepinte to Castelnaudari 2 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 88]

Monday, 29 March 1677, n.s.

19 March 1677, o.s.

“From Castelnaudary to Montesquieu by the Canal 5 leagues. … From Montesquieu to Tholose 5 leagues by the Canale.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 89]

Wednesday, 31 March 1677, n.s.

21 March 1677, o.s.

“From Tholouse to Verdune 5 leagues upon the Garonne.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 2, p. 95]
