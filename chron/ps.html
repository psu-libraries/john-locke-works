Saint-Maximin

1676 Apr 6/16: Locke traveled from Sainte-Baum to Saint-Maximin.

1676 Apr 6/16: Locke made notes on the weather at Saint-Maximin.

Saint-Vallier

1675 Dec 17/27: Locke rode from Vienne to Saint-Vallier.

Sainte-Baum

1676 Apr 5/15: Locke traveled from Hyères through Solliès and Belgentier [?] to Méounes, and on to Sainte-Baum.

Salisbury

1667 June 22: Letter to Locke from Dr. David Thomas at Salisbury.

1667 Nov 29: Letter to Locke from Dr. David Thomas at Salisbury.

1669 Oct 19: Letter to Locke from Dr. David Thomas at Salisbury.

1671 Oct 10: Letter to Dr. John Mapletoft from Locke at Salisbury.

1671 ca. Oct 20: Locke leaves Salisbury for Sutton.

1673 Sep 29: Letter to Locke from Dr. David Thomas at Salisbury.

1677 Jul 21/31: Letter to Locke from Dr. David Thomas at Salisbury.

Salon

1676 Mar 29/Apr 8: Locke traveled from Arles to St. Martin-de-Crau, and on to Salon.

Sète

1676 Feb 23/Mar 4: Locke rode from Montpellier to Balaruc-les-Bains, through Frontignan and Sète.

Solliès

1676 Apr 5/15: Locke traveled from Hyères through Solliès and Belgentier [?] to Méounes, and on to Sainte-Baum.

St. Gilles

1676 Mar 27/Apr 6: Locke traveled from Montpellier to Pont Lunel, and on to Aigues-Mortes, Vauvert and St. Gilles.

St. Martin-de-Crau

1676 Mar 29/Apr 8: Locke traveled from Arles to St. Martin-de-Crau, and on to Salon.
