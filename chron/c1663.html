1663

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Thursday, 15 January 1663, o.s.

25 January 1663, n.s.

Letter: From Sir Charles Berkeley, dated 15 January [1663?]; the year is supplied by De Beer; written from Trinity College Cambridge; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 153]

Person encountered: Sir Charles Berkeley (letter)

Tuesday, 20 January 1663, o.s.

30 January 1663, n.s.

Letter: From Thomas Harborne, dated 20 January 1662[/63]; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 154]

Person encountered: Thomas Harborne (letter)

February

Wednsday, 4 February 1663, o.s.

14 February 1663, n.s.

Letter: From Henry Townshend, dated 4 February 1662[/63]; with a postscript of accounts; written from Worcester. [De Beer, i. 155; postscript ix. 155]

Person encountered: Henry Townshend (letter).

April

Tuesday, 14 April 1663, o.s.

24 April 1663, n.s.

Letter: From Sir Charles Berkeley, dated 14 April 1663; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 156]

Person encountered: Sir Charles Berkeley (letter)

Friday, 17 April 1663, o.s. [Good Friday]

27 April 1663, n.s.

Letter: From George Williamson, dated 17 April 1663; written from Bristol; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 157]

Person encountered: George Williamson (letter)

Sunday, 19 April 1663, o.s.

29 April 1663, n.s.

Easter [o.s.]

Wednesday, 29 April 1663, o.s.

9 May 1663, n.s.

[At Oxford].

Letters:

  • To William Uvedale, dated 29 April 1663; written from Christ Church Oxford; addressed to Uvedale in London. [De Beer, i. 158]
  • From Richard Williamson, dated 29 April 1663; written from Bristol; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 159]

Persons encountered: William Uvedale (letter); Richard Williamson (letter)

  ▲  

May

Date Uncertain

Letter: From Sir Charles Berkeley, [late May 1663]; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 160]

Person encountered: Sir Charles Berkeley (letter)

July

Wednesday, 15 July 1663, o.s.

25 July 1663, n.s.

Letter: To Henry Barnard, dated 15 July 1663 (missing); answered by Barnard on 20 October. [see De Beer, i. 162]

August

Saturday, 15 August 1663, o.s.

25 August 1663, n.s.

Letter: From Sir Charles Berkeley, datd 15 August 1663; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 161]

Person encountered: Sir Charles Berkeley (letter)

Saturday, 29 August 1663, o.s.

8 September 1663, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 31.

October

Tuesday, 20 October 1663, o.s.

30 October 1663, n.s.

Letter: From Henry Barnard, dated 20 October 1663; written from Bridgenorth; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. Answers Locke’s letter of 15 July (missing). [De Beer, i. 162]

Person encountered: Henry Barnard (letter)

November

Wednesday, 18 November 1663, o.s.

28 November 1663, n.s.

Letter: From John Strachey, dated 18 November 1663. [De Beer, i. 163]

Person encountered: John Strachey (letter)

December

Saturday, 26 December 1663, o.s.

7 January 1664, n.s.

Letter: From William Owen, dated 26 December 1663; written from Pontesbury; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 164]

Person encountered: William Owen (letter)

Date Uncertain

Manuscript:   Essays on the law of nature. Written ca. 1663-1664; later copies date from 1664 and (possibly) 1681.
