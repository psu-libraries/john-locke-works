1663

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Thursday, 15 January 1663, o.s.

25 January 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From Sir Charles Berkeley; the year is supplied by De Beer; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 153]

Tuesday, 20 January 1663, o.s.

30 January 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From Thomas Harborne; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 154]

February

Wednsday, 4 February 1663, o.s.

14 February 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From Henry Townshend; with a postscript of accounts. [De Beer, i. 155; postscript ix. 155]

April

Tuesday, 14 April 1663, o.s.

24 April 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From Sir Charles Berkeley; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 156]

Friday, 17 April 1663, o.s. [Good Friday]

27 April 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From George Williamson, Bristol; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 157]

Sunday, 19 April 1663, o.s.

29 April 1663, n.s.

Easter [o.s.]

Wednesday, 29 April 1663, o.s.

9 May 1663, n.s.

[At Oxford].

Letters dated: To William Uvedale, London. [De Beer, i. 157]; from Richard Williamson, Bristol. [De Beer, i. 159]

  ▲  

August

Saturday, 15 August 1663, o.s.

25 August 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From Sir Charles Berkeley; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 161]

Saturday, 29 August 1663, o.s.

8 September 1663, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 31.

October

Tuesday, 20 October 1663, o.s.

30 October 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From Henry Barnard, Bridgenorth; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 162]

November

Wednesday, 18 November 1663, o.s.

28 November 1663, n.s.

Letter dated: From John Strachey. [De Beer, i. 163]

December

Saturday, 26 December 1663, o.s.

7 January 1664, n.s.

Letter dated: From William Owen, Pontesbury; addressed to Locke at Christ Church. [De Beer, i. 164]

Undated

Work written (ca. 1663-1664):   Essays on the law of nature. Later copies date from 1664 and (possibly) 1681.

Sources:
