January | February | March | April | May | June

January

Sunday, 31 January 1664, o.s.

10 February 1664, n.s.

Letter: From William Coker (copy), dated 31 January 1664. [De Beer, i. 165 (not printed)]

Person encountered: William Coker (letter)

February

Sunday, 21 February 1664, o.s.

2 March 1664, n.s.

Letter: From William Carr, dated 2 March [1664] (n.s.); year based on the contents of the letter. Written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 166]

Person encountered: William Carr (letter)

April

Friday, 29 April 1664, o.s.

9 May 1664, n.s.

Letter: From Charles Powell, dated 29 April 1664; written from Bristol; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 167]

Person encountered: Charles Powell (letter)

  ▲  

May

Tuesday, 24 May 1664, o.s.

3 June 1664, n.s.

Letter: From Sir Charles Berkeley, dated 24 May 1664; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 167]

Person encountered: Sir Charles Berkeley (letter)

August

Monday, 29 August 1664, o.s.

8 September 1664, n.s.

Locke’s birthday, age 32.

December

Wednesday, 14 December 1664, o.s.

24 December 1664, n.s.

Letter: From William Coker (copy), dated 14 December 1664. [De Beer, i. 169 (not printed)]

Person encountered: William Coker (letter)

Date Uncertain

Manuscript:   Valedictory speech as Censor of Moral Philosophy (ca. December 1664).

Date Uncertain

Manuscript: (1664?):   Essays on the law of nature. Originally written ca. 1663-1664; “MS B” [MS. Locke f. 31, ff. 9-119] ends “Sic cogitavit J. Locke 1664”.
