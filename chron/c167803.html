Tuesday, 1 March 1678, n.s.

19 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Patta

Wednesday, 2 March 1678, n.s.

20 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Maimonides — [Lough “Reading”]

Thursday, 3 March 1678, n.s.

21 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: To Robert Huntington, dated 3 March 1678 n.s. (lost) — [see Corr., i. 382]

Friday, 4 March 1678, n.s.

22 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Gold, Rohault — [Lough “Reading”]

Saturday, 5 March 1678, n.s.

23 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Chaillou, Varice, Menstrua, Lac, Febris lactea, Fermentation, Plinie, Wine, Intermittentes, Quartana [twice], Vomitoria — [Lough “Reading”, Dewhurst]

Letter: To Sir John Banks, dated 5 March 1678 n.s. (lost); answered by Banks on 11/21 March 1678. [see Corr., i. 373]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

  ▲  

Monday, 7 March 1678, n.s.

25 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Herpes, Diarrhaea, Cartesian philosophie [in French], Cartesian [repeated on pages 50 and 52; in French], Cartesianisme [repeated on pages 54, 56, 58 and 60; in French] — [Dewhurst, Aaron & Gibb]

Tuesday, 8 March 1678, n.s.

26 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Shaftesbury released from the Tower, where he had been sent on 16 February 1677 [o.s.].

Journal entry: Diarrhaea — [Dewhurst]

Letter: From Dr. John Mapletoft, dated 26 February 1678 o.s.; written from Gresham College, London; addressed to Locke in Paris. [Corr., i. 371]

Persons encountered: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Wednesday, 9 March 1678, n.s.

27 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Diarrhaea — [Dewhurst]

Thursday, 10 March 1678, n.s.

28 February 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Diarrhaea — [Dewhurst]

Saturday, 12 March 1678, n.s.

2 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Stringer, E. Shaftesbury, Mapletoft, Diarrhaea, Ramusio, Leo Afr:, B [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough, Dewhurst, Lough “Reading”]

Letter: From Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 12 March 1678 n.s.; written from Aix; with three enclosed papers; answered by Locke on 13/23 March 1678. [Corr., i. 366]

Person encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter)

  ▲  

Sunday, 13 March 1678, n.s.

3 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Diarrhaea — [Dewhurst]

Saturday, 19 March 1678, n.s.

9 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Figues

Sunday, 20 March 1678, n.s.

10 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Scrupulos, Scrupulosity — [King]

Monday, 21 March 1678, n.s.

11 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 11 March 1678 o.s.; writteen from London; answers lost letters from Locke of 9/19 February and 23 February/5 March 1678. [Corr., i. 373]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Tuesday, 22 March 1678, n.s.

12 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Versailes, B [i.e. Caleb Banks] — [Lough]

Wednesday, 23 March 1678, n.s.

13 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Dysenteria — [Dewhurst]

Letter: To Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 23 March 1678 n.s.; written from Paris; answers Grenville’s letter of 2/12 March 1678; draft in Journal dated 10/20 March 1678 (“Scrupulosity”). [Corr., i. 374]

Person encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter)

  ▲  

Monday, 28 March 1678, n.s.

18 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Strumae, Corrosivu[m], Dysenteria, Diarrhaea, Hysterica, Fortune, Cailliere — [Dewhurst, Lough “Reading”]

Tuesday, 29 March 1678, n.s.

19 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Pyrard, Measures, Piens, Moul, Larin, Dysenteria, Diarrhaea — [Lough “Reading”, Dewhurst]

Wednesday, 30 March 1678, n.s.

20 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Letter: To Sir John Banks, dated 30 March 1678 n.s. (lost); answered by Banks on 1/11 April 1678. [see Corr., i. 376]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Thursday, 31 March 1678, n.s.

21 March 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Atlantis, Dysenteria, Capillus rufus — [Bellatella, Dewhurst, Lough]
