Friday, 3 July 1676, n.s.

23 June 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entruies: Maimonides, Harvest, Thrashing — [Lough]

Saturday, 4 July 1676, n.s.

24 June 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entries: Broughton, Cotton, Essences [i.e. Scents] — [Lough]

  ▲  

Sunday, 5 July 1676, n.s.

25 June 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entry: Broughton — [Lough]

Wednesday, 8 July 1676, n.s.

28 June 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Letters:

  • From Mrs. Anna Grigg, dated 28 June 1676 o.s.. [Corr., i. 313]
  • From Dr. John Mapletoft, dated 28 June 1676 o.s.. [Corr., i. 314]

Thursday, 9 July 1676, n.s.

29 June 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entries: Hysterica [repeated on pages 311-312], Extension [repeated on pages 313-314], Serge d’Uzes — [Dewhurst, Von Leyden, Lough]

Friday, 10 July 1676, n.s.

30 June 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entries: Morsus viparae, Inflammatio, Payments — [Dewhurst, Lough]

Saturday, 11 July 1676, n.s.

1 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entry: Vinum — [Lough]

  ▲  

Sunday, 12 July 1676, n.s.

2 July 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Celleneuve: “So cold that a fire had done no harm.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; shorthand]

Monday, 13 July 1676, n.s.

3 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entries: Simple Ideas, Will, Pleasure, Pain — [Aaron & Gibb]

Tuesday, 14 July 1676, n.s.

4 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entries: Atlantis, Livre — [Goldie, Lough]

Wednesday, 15 July 1676, n.s.

5 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entries: Mania, Fatuitas, Idolatry [repeated on pages 320, 322, 324] — [Dewhurst, Von Leyden]

Thursday, 16 July 1676, n.s.

6 July 1676, o.s.

Weather observations at Celleneuve: “Fair and so cold that one had wished it to be more warm.” — [Journal, ed. Lough; part shorthand]

Journal entries: States [Estates of Languedoc], Passions, Love [repeated on pages 326, 328], Desire [repeated on pages 331, 339-340, 342], Hope, Hatred, Pain, Pleasure [repeated on pages 333, 338], Weariness, Vexation, Sorrow, Greif, Torment, Melancholy, Anxiety, Anguish, Misery [repeated on pages 334, 336], Mirth, Delight, Joy, Comfort, Happynesse [repeated on pages 335-336], Bonum, Power [repeated on pages 343-344], Will — [Lough, Von Leyden]

Saturday, 18 July 1676, n.s.

8 July 1676, o.s.

At Celleneuve.

Journal entries: Figues, Taxes, Measures, Increase, Wheat, Mr Wall, Silk stockings, Venerea, Collot, Struma — [Lough, Dewhurst]

Letter: From Thomas Stringer, dated 8 July 1676 o.s.; written from Bexwells; addressed to Locke at Montpellier; received 24 July/3 August. [Corr., i. 315]
