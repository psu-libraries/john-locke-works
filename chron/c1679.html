22 Dec. 1678, o.s.

[At Paris].

March

April

May

Tuesday, 2 May 1679, n.s.

22 April 1679, o.s.

“From Paris to Clermont 14 leagues.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 90]

Wednesday, 3 May 1679, n.s.

23 April 1679, o.s.

“From Clermont to Amiens 14 leagues …” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 90]

Thursday, 4 May 1679, n.s.

24 April 1679, o.s.

“To Abbeville 10 leagues.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 91]

Friday, 5 May 1679, n.s.

25 April 1679, o.s.

“To Montriel [Montreuil] 10 leagues & from thence to Boulogne 7 leagues.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 92]

Saturday, 6 May 1679, n.s.

26 April 1679, o.s.

“To Calais 7 leagues.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 92]

Monday, 8 May 1679, n.s.

28 April 1679, o.s.

“Sailed at 11 a clocke in the Charlotte, yacht, Captain Sanderson commander.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 92]

Tuesday, 9 May 1679, n.s.

29 April 1679, o.s.

“In the Thames over against Lee [Leigh] where we lay for want of winde …” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, pp. 92-93]

Wednesday, 10 May 1679, n.s.

30 April 1679, o.s.

“Landed at the Temple.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 93]

June

Tuesday, 17 June 1679, o.s.

27 June 1679, n.s.

[From London to Bexwells (home of Thomas Stringer)].

Wednesday, 18 June 1679, o.s.

28 June 1679, n.s.

“From Bexwells to London 25 m.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 103]

July

August

Monday, 18 August 1679, o.s.

28 August 1679, n.s.

“From London to Rochester 26. From Rochester to Sitingburn 11. To Leeds Cast[le]. To Olantigh 7” (home of Sir John Banks). — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 133]

September

Monday, 29 September 1679, o.s.

6 October 1679, n.s.

“From Olanty to Canterbury 8 miles.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 158]

Tuesday, 30 September 1679, o.s.

7 October 1679, n.s.

“To Chilham 3 m. [and back to Olantigh?].” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 159]

October

Thursday, 2 October 1679, o.s.

12 October 1679, n.s.

“From Olanty to Alsford 22 miles.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 159]

Friday, 3 October 1679, o.s.

13 October 1679, n.s.

“To London 32 miles.” — [Journal: British Library, Add. MS. 15,642, p. 159]
