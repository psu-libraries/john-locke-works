July

Saturday, 9 July 1678, n.s.

29 June 1678, o.s.

“From Paris to Longemeaux [Longjumeau] 4 Leagues, about a mile from which on the top of the hill is the Chasteau de Chayly [Chilly] …” [From thence to Toury] — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 190]

Monday, 11 July 1678, n.s.

1 July 1678, o.s.

“From Toury to Orleans 10 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 191]

“From Orleans to Avery 8 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 227]

“From Avery to Bloys 7 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 229]

August

Friday, 5 August 1678, n.s.

26 July 1678, o.s.

“We saw Menars, a fine house … situated about 2 leagues above Bloys … We also say Chambort … about 3 leagues from Bloys … We also saw Beauregard, a litle house … about 1½ leagues from Bloys.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, pp. 233-236]

Monday, 8 August 1678, n.s.

29 July 1678, o.s.

“From Bloys to Tours 16 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 241]

Wednesday, 10 August 1678, n.s.

31 July 1678, o.s.

“From Tours to Verret 2 leagues … [returned to Tours]” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 243]

Friday, 12 August 1678, n.s.

2 August 1678, o.s.

“From Tours to Saumur 17 leagues. cariage by water in a boat by our selves …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 252]

Friday, 19 August 1678, n.s.

9 August 1678, o.s.

“From Saumur to Angers 10 leagues …” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 256]

Friday, 26 August 1678, n.s.

16 August 1678, o.s.

“We saw the quarrys of Ardoise.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 264]

Monday, 29 August 1678, n.s.

19 August 1678, o.s.

“From Angers to Saumur 10 litle leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 270]

Tuesday, 30 August 1678, n.s.

20 August 1678, o.s.

“From Saumur to Chinon 6 leagues. … From Chinon to Richelieu 4 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, pp. 271-272]

Wednesday, 31 August 1678, n.s.

21 August 1678, o.s.

“From Richleiue [sic] we went to Milleray [La Meilleraye] 11 or 12 leagues.” — [Journal: MS. Locke f. 3, p. 275]
