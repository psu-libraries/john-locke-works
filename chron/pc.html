Calais

Cambridge University

See also Trinity College

Chagny

1675 Dec 8/18: Locke traveled from Arnay-le-Duc to Chagny and on to Châlons-sur-Saône.

Châlons-sur-Saône

1675 Dec 8/18: Locke traveled from Arnay-le-Duc to Chagny and on to Châlons-sur-Saône.

1675 Dec 9/19: Locke traveled by boat from Châlons-sur-Saône to Tournus and on to Mascon.

Charleston

1673 June 28: Letter to Locke from Joseph West at Charleston.

Châteauneuf-sur-Rhône

1675 Dec 20/30: Locke rode from Montélimar to Pont St Esprit, by way of Châteauneuf-sur-Rhône and Lapalud, and on to Orange.

Cleves

1665 Nov 11: Passport issued to Sir Walter Vane for his mission to Cleves.

1665 Nov 13: Locke leaves Oxford en route to Cleves.

1665 Dec 1/11: Sir Walter Vane and his train (including Locke) arrive at Cleves.

1665 Dec 9/19: Locke dines with the Franciscan Friars at Cleves.

1665 Dec 10/20: Locke attends services in the Lutheran church in Cleves.

1665 Dec 12/22: Letter to Robert Boyle from Locke at Cleves.

1665 Dec 15/25: Christmas in Cleves; Locke attends services in the (Catholic) Stiftskirche and the Carthusian church.

1665 Dec 19/29: Letter to William Godolphin from Locke at Cleves.

1665 Dec 24/1666 Jan 4: Locke attends services in the Lutheran church in Cleves, and observes a baptism at the Calvinist church.

1665 Dec 30/1666 Jan 9: Letter to William Godolphin from Locke at Cleves.

1666 Jan 16/26: Letter to Joseph Williamson from Locke at Cleves.

1666 Jan 19/29: Letter to William Godolphin from Locke at Cleves.

1666 Apr 17/27: Letter from Franciscus Raulin, S.J., at Cleves to [Locke?].

1666 May 10/20: Letter to Locke from J. Schard at Cleves.
