Sunday, 1 May 1678, n.s.

21 April 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entry: Venera salivatio — [Dewhurst]

Letter (undated): From William Charleton; torn corner. — [De Beer, i. 379]

Monday, 2 May 1678, n.s.

22 April 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Viperae, Stringer — [Dewhurst, Lough].

Letter received (?): From Thomas Stringer; dated 15 April. — [De Beer, i. 378]

23 April 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Friday, 6 May 1678, n.s.

26 April 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Arrobe, Quintal, Oculi, Salivatio — [Dewhurst, Lough “Reading”].

Saturday, 7 May 1678, n.s.

27 April 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Denarius, Sestertius, Nummus — [Lough “Reading”].

Journal entries: As, Sestertius, Denarius, Silver, Salivatio — [Dewhurst].

Tuesday, 17 May 1678, n.s.

7 May 1678, o.s.

Weather observations at Paris: “Till the beginning of this weeke the weather hath been wet, cold & winterly. Now it begins to be warme.” — [Journal, ed. Lough]

Journal entries: Kircher, Clavius, Arriaga, Fornerius, Mersennus, Magnanus, Bayle, Massaria, Tithes — [Lough “Reading”, Dewhurst].

Monday, 23 May 1678, n.s.

13 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Salivatio, Guns — [Dewhurst, Lough].

Letter dated: From Sir John Banks, London. — [De Beer, i. 381]

Tuesday, 24 May 1678, n.s.

14 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Locke today observed a case at the Charité, concerning “one who had horny excresencies or extraordinary large nails on his fingers and toes.” Locke’s account is repeated in a letter to Boyle of 6 August and was published in the Philosophical Transactions, vol. XIX (1697), pages 594–596.

Journal entries: Cornua digitorum [repeated on pages 137 and 138], Calculus, Pes philosophihcus PP, Salivatio — [Dewhurst].

At Paris.

Journal entry: Jesuits — [Lough “Reading”].

16 May 1678, o.s.

At Paris.

Journal entries: Salivatio, Garde de meubles, Atlantis — [Dewhurst, Lough, Bellatella].
