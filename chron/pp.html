Pall Mall, London

1675 May 20: Letter from Locke to Henry Oldenburg at his house in Pall Mall.

Paris

1672 Sep 24/Oct 4: Locke arrived in Paris.

1672 before Oct 13: Locke returned to London from Paris.

1675 Nov 24/Dec 4: Locke traveled from Tillart to Beaumont and on the Paris; he remained in Paris until 4/14 December.

1675 4/Dec 14: Locke left Paris on the Lyon coach, traveling to Melun and on to Moret.

Peccais

1676 Mar 15/25: Locke traveled from Aigues-Mortes to Peccais, and on to Pont Lunel.

Pensford

1652 May 4: Letter written by Locke to his father at Pensford.

1652 May 11: Letter written by Locke to his father at Pensford.

1654 Jan-Feb?: Letter from Locke to “N” at Pensford.

1655 Aug 2: Letter to Locke to Thomas Grenfeild at Pensford.

1655 early Sep?: Letter from Locke at Pensford to [Samuel Tilly?].

1655 Sep 11: Letter to Locke at Pensford from Samuel Tilly.

1656 summer?: Letter to Locke at Pensford from Francis Atkins.

1656 Oct 25: Letter from Locke to his father at Pensford.

1657 Apr 15: Letter to Locke at Pensford from Francis Atkins.

1658 Mar 18: Letter to Locke from Thomas Grenfeild at Pensford.

1658 Mar 18: Letter to Locke from Thomas Grenfeild at Pensford.

1659 late May?: Letter from an unknown correspondent to another unknown correspondent at Pensford.

1659 Sep 19: Draft letter from Locke at Pensford to an unidentified lady.

1659 Oct 20: Letter from Locke at Pensford to Tom [Westrowe?].

1659 Nov 8: Letter from Locke at Pensford to [Thomas Westrowe?].

1659 Nov 8: Letter from Anne Evelegh to Locke at Pensford.

1659 Nov 22: Letter from Anne Evelegh to Locke at Pensford.

1659 Dec 12: Letter to an unidentified lady from Locke at Ditcheat, near Pensford.

1660 ca. Jan 9: Letter from Locke to his father at Pensford.

1660 May 24: Letter from John Strachey to John Locke, sen., at Pensford.

1660 Oct 23: Letter from Gabriel Towerson to Locke at Pensford.

1660 ca. Oct 30?: Letter from William Uvedale to Locke at Pensford.

1660 ca. Nov 3?: Letter from Gabriel Towerson to Locke at Pensford.

1660 Dec 20?: Letter from Locke to his father at Pensford.

1661 Jan 2: Letter from Dr. Edmund Meara to Locke at Pensford.

1661 Jan 8?: Letter from Locke at Pensford to Dr. Ayliffe Ivye.

1661 Feb 19: Letter from Robert Crosse to Locke at Pensford.

1661 Apr 2: Letters from Mrs. Elizabeth Jepp and John Strachey to Locke at Pensford.

1661 Apr 9: Letter from Gabriel Towerson to Locke at [Pensford].

1666 Apr 10?: Letter from [Elinor Parry?] to Locke at Pensford.

Petworth, Sussex

1670 Sep 12: Letter to Locke from Mrs. Margaret Blomer at Petworth.

1670 Sep 23: Letter to Locke from Mrs. Margaret Blomer at Petworth.

1671 July 10: Letter to Locke from Mrs. Margaret Blomer at Petworth.

1671 July 14: Letter to Locke from Lady Ashley at Petworth.

Poix

1675 Nov 22/Dec 2: Locke traveled from Abbeville to Poix.

Pont du Gard

1675 Dec 23/1676 Jan 2: Locke traveled from Avignon to Pont du Gard.

Pont Lunel

1675 Dec 25/1676 Jan 4: Locke traveled from Nîmes to Montpellier, through Pont Lunel.

1676 Mar 14/24: Locke traveled from Montpellier to Pont Lunel, and on to Aigues-Mortes.

1676 Mar 14/24: Locke made notes on the weather at Montpellier.

1676 Mar 15/25: Locke traveled from Aigues-Mortes to Peccais, and on to Pont Lunel.

1676 Mar 15/25: Locke made notes on the weather at Montpellier.

1676 Mar 27/Apr 6: Locke traveled from Montpellier to Pont Lunel, and on to Aigues-Mortes, Vauvert and St. Gilles.

1676 Apr 13/23: Locke traveled from Tarascon to Générac, and on to Pont Lunel.

Pont St. Esprit

1675 Dec 20/30: Locke rode from Montélimar to Pont St Esprit, by way of Châteauneuf-sur-Rhône and Lapalud, and on to Orange.

Pont-sur-Yonne

1675 Dec 5/155: Locke traveled from Moret to Pont sur Yonne and on to Joigny.

Pontesbury, Shropshire

1663 Dec 26: Letter to Locke from William Owen at Pontesbury.
