Pensford

1652 May 4: Letter written by Locke to his father at Pensford.

1652 May 11: Letter written by Locke to his father at Pensford.

1654 Jan-Feb?: Letter from Locke to “N” at Pensford.

1655 Aug 2: Letter to Locke to Thomas Grenfeild at Pensford.

1655 early Sep?: Letter from Locke at Pensford to [Samuel Tilly?].

1655 Sep 11: Letter to Locke at Pensford from Samuel Tilly.

1656 summer?: Letter to Locke at Pensford from Francis Atkins.

1656 Oct 25: Letter from Locke to his father at Pensford.

1657 Apr 15: Letter to Locke at Pensford from Francis Atkins.

1658 Mar 18: Letter to Locke from Thomas Grenfeild at Pensford.

1659 June 22: Letter from Locke to his father at Pensford.

1659 Sep 19: Draft letter from Locke at Pensford to an unidentified lady.

1659 Oct 20: Letter from Locke at Pensford to Tom [Westrowe?].

1659 Nov 8: Letter from Locke at Pensford to [Thomas Westrowe?].

1659 Nov 8: Letter from Anne Evelegh to Locke at Pensford.

1659 Nov 22: Letter from Anne Evelegh to Locke at Pensford.

1659 Dec 12: Letter to an unidentified lady from Locke at Ditcheat, near Pensford.

1660 ca. Jan 9: Letter from Locke to his father at Pensford.

1660 May 24: Letter from John Strachey to John Locke, sen., at Pensford.

1660 Oct 23: Letter from Gabriel Towerson to Locke at Pensford.

1660 ca. Oct 30?: Letter from William Uvedale to Locke at Pensford.

1660 ca. Nov 3?: Letter from Gabriel Towerson to Locke at Pensford.

1660 Dec 20?: Letter from Locke to his father at Pensford.

1661 Jan 2: Letter from Dr. Edmund Meara to Locke at Pensford.

1661 Jan 8?: Letter from Locke at Pensford to Dr. Ayliffe Ivye.

1661 Feb 19: Letter from Robert Crosse to Locke at Pensford.

1661 Apr 2: Letters from Mrs. Elizabeth Jepp and John Strachey to Locke at Pensford.

1661 Apr 9: Letter from Gabriel Towerson to Locke at [Pensford].

Pontesbury, Shropshire

1663 Dec 26: Letter to Locke from William Owen at Pontesbury.
