Pall Mall, London

1675 May 20: Letter from Locke to Henry Oldenburg at his house in Pall Mall.

1677 Dec 1/11: Letter to Dr. John Mapletoft from Dr. Thomas Sydenham in Pall Mall.

1678 Aug 3/13: Letter to Locke from Dr. Thomas Sydenham at Pall Mall.

Paris

1672 Sep 24/Oct 4: Locke arrived in Paris.

1672 before Oct 13: Locke returned to London from Paris.

1675 Nov 24/Dec 4: Locke traveled from Tillart to Beaumont and on the Paris; he remained in Paris until 4/14 December.

1675 4/Dec 14: Locke left Paris on the Lyon coach, traveling to Melun and on to Moret.

Poix

Pont Lunel

1675 Dec 25/1676 Jan 4: Locke traveled from Nîmes to Montpellier, through Pont Lunel.

1676 Mar 14/24: Locke traveled from Montpellier to Pont Lunel, and on to Aigues-Mortes.

1676 Mar 14/24: Locke made notes on the weather at Montpellier.

1676 Mar 15/25: Locke made notes on the weather at Montpellier.

1676 Mar 27/Apr 6: Locke traveled from Montpellier to Pont Lunel, and on to Aigues-Mortes, Vauvert and St. Gilles.

1676 Apr 13/23: Locke traveled from Tarascon to Générac, and on to Pont Lunel.

Pont St. Esprit

1675 Dec 20/30: Locke rode from Montélimar to Pont St Esprit, by way of Châteauneuf-sur-Rhône and Lapalud, and on to Orange.

Pont-sur-Yonne

1675 Dec 5/155: Locke traveled from Moret to Pont sur Yonne and on to Joigny.

Pontesbury, Shropshire

1663 Dec 26: Letter to Locke from William Owen at Pontesbury.
