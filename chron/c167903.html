Wednesday, 1 March 1679, n.s.

19 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Friday, 3 March 1679, n.s.

21 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: To Jacques Selapris, dated 3 March 1679 n.s. (lost); answered by Selapris on 2/12 March 1679. [See Corr., i. 451]

Person encountered: Jacques Selapris (letter)

Friday, 10 March 1679, n.s.

28 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: To Thomas Tufton, dated 10 March 1679 n.s. (lost); answered by Tufton on 11/21 March 1679. [See Corr., i. 451]

Persons encountered: Thomas Tufton, later Earl of Thanet (letter)

Saturday, 11 March 1679, n.s.

1 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Dr. John Mapletoft, written ca. 11 March 1679 n.s.. [Corr., i. 450]

Person encountered: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Sunday, 12 March 1679, n.s.

2 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letters:

  • From Jacques Selapris, dated 12 March 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers three letters dated 3 February and 3 March 1679. [Corr., i. 451]
  • From Thomas Herbert, dated 2 March 1679 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 452]

Person encountered: Thomas Herbert, later Earl of Pembroke (letter), Jacques Selapris (letter)

Tuesday, 14 March 1679, n.s.

4 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: To Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 14 March 1679 n.s. (lost); answered by Grenville on 22 March/1 April 1679. [See Corr., i. 458]

Person encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter)

Saturday, 18 March 1679, n.s.

8 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Edward Bernard, dated 8 March 1678/9 o.s. written from Oxford; addressed to Locke via Sir John Banks, London. [Corr., i. 453]

Person encountered: Edward Bernard (letter)

Tuesday, 21 March 1679, n.s.

11 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Thomas Tufton, dated 21 March 1679 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “your kind letter last Post of the 10 of marche” (lost). [Corr., i. 454]

Persons encountered: Thomas Tufton, later Earl of Thanet (letter)

Wednesday, 22 March 1679, n.s.

12 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 12 March 1679 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London. [Corr., i. 455]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Wednesday, 29 March 1679, n.s.

19 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: To Sir John Banks, dated 29 March 1679 n.s. (lost); answered by Banks on 31 March/10 April 1679. [See Corr., i. 461]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Thursday, 30 March 1679, n.s.

20 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 20 March 1679 o.s.; written from London; copy by an amanuensis prefixed to Banks’s letter of 27 March/6 April; Locke may have failed to receive the original. [Corr., i. 456]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)
