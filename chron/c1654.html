January

Date uncertain

Letter: To “N”, [January-February 1654?]; written from Oxford to someone in Pensford.   [De Beer, i. 12]

Person encountered: “N” (letter)

May

Monday, 1 May 1654, o.s.

11 May 1654, n.s.

Letters: Three letters to an unknown correspondent. The first letter was probably intended to be sent. On the verso is a draft of a second letter, (or perhaps for another version of the same letter); these two letters are endorsed “Letters to H. C”. The third letter appears to be a related draft. The first and third letters are dated 1 May 1654, from Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 15–17]

Person encountered: “H. C” (letter)

  ▲  

June

Date uncertain

Publication: Musarum Oxoniensium Έλαιοφορία, published ca. June 1654; included two poems by Locke (his first published works).

August

Sunday, 29 August 1654, o.s.

8 September 1654, n.s.

Locke’s birthday; age 22.

Date Uncertain

Letters:

  • To “A M.”, [early 1654?]. [De Beer, i. 13]
  • To “B E”, [1654 or 1655?]. [De Beer, i. 14]

Persons encountered: “A. M.” (letter); “B E” (letter)
