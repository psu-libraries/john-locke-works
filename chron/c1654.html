January

Date uncertain

May

Monday, 1 May 1654, o.s.

11 May 1654, n.s.

[At Oxford].

Letters written: Three letters to an unknown correspondent. The first letter was probably intended to be sent. On the verso is a draft, perhaps for another version of the same letter; these two letters are endorsed “Letters to H. C“ The third letter appears to be a related draft. The first and third letters are dated. [De Beer, i. 15–17]
