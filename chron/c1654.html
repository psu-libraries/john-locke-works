January

Date uncertain

Letter (January-February 1654?): To “N.” [De Beer, i. 12]

May

Monday, 1 May 1654, o.s.

11 May 1654, n.s.

[At Oxford].

Letters dated: Three letters to an unknown correspondent. The first letter was probably intended to be sent. On the verso is a draft, perhaps for another version of the same letter; these two letters are endorsed “Letters to H. C“ The third letter appears to be a related draft. The first and third letters are dated. [De Beer, i. 15–17]

Undated

Letter (early 1654?): To “A M”. [De Beer, i. 13]

Letter (1654 or 1655?): To “B E”. [De Beer, i. 14]
