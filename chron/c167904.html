Wednesday, 1 April 1679, n.s.

22 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letters:

  • From Jacques Selapris, dated 1 April 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 457]

Person encountered: Jacques Selapris (letter)

==============================================================

Friday, 10 March 1679, n.s.

28 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: To Thomas Tufton, dated 10 March 1679 n.s. (lost); answered by Tufton on 11/21 March 1679. [See Corr., i. 451]

Persons encountered: Thomas Tufton, later Earl of Thanet (letter)

Saturday, 11 March 1679, n.s.

1 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Dr. John Mapletoft, written ca. 11 March 1679 n.s.. [Corr., i. 450]

Person encountered: Dr. John Mapletoft (letter)

Sunday, 12 March 1679, n.s.

2 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letters:

  • From Jacques Selapris, dated 12 March 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers three letters dated 3 February and 3 March 1679. [Corr., i. 451]
  • From Thomas Herbert, dated 2 March 1679 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 452]

Person encountered: Thomas Herbert, later Earl of Pembroke (letter), Jacques Selapris (letter)

Saturday, 8 March 1679, n.s.

18 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Edward Bernard, dated 8 March 1678/9 o.s. written from Oxford; addressed to Locke via Sir John Banks, London. [Corr., i. 453]

Person encountered: Edward Bernard (letter)

Tuesday, 21 March 1679, n.s.

11 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Thomas Tufton, dated 21 March 1679 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “your kind letter last Post of the 10 of marche” (lost). [Corr., i. 454]

Persons encountered: Thomas Tufton, later Earl of Thanet (letter)

Wednesday, 22 March 1679, n.s.

12 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 12 March 1679 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London. [Corr., i. 455]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Thursday, 30 March 1679, n.s.

20 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 20 March 1679 o.s.; written from London; copy by an amanuensis prefixed to Banks’s letter of 27 March/6 April; Locke may have failed to receive the original. [Corr., i. 456]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Letter: From Peter Locke, dated 22 January 1679 o.s.; written from Sutton; answers “yours of 30 dec:”. [Corr., i. 434]

Person encountered: Peter Locke (letter)

Thursday, 2 February 1679, n.s.

23 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 23 January 1679 o.s.; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 444]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Tuesday, 14 February 1679, n.s.

4 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From William Charleton, dated 14 February 1679 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke at Paris; with enclosures: (1) a catalogue of seeds; (2) an inventory of “things sent from Montpellier 14 Feb. 79”. [Corr., i. 445]

Person encountered: William Charleton (letter)

Sunday, 19 February 1679, n.s.

9 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Jacques Horutener and Jacques Selapris, dated 19 February 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 446]

Person encountered: Jacques Horutener (letter), Jacques Selapris (letter)

Monday, 20 February 1679, n.s.

10 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 20 February 1679 n.s.; written from La Tour d’Aigues; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “yours of Jan. the 27th” (lost). [Corr., i. 447]

Persons encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter)

Tuesday, 21 February 1679, n.s.

11 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Thomas Tufton, dated 21 February 1679 n.s.; written from Montpellier; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “your last letter dated the 27th of jannuary” (lost). [Corr., i. 448]

Persons encountered: Thomas Tufton, later Earl of Thanet (letter)

Tuesday, 28 February 1679, n.s.

18 February 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From William Charleton, dated 28 February 1679 n.s.; written from Montpellier; with enclosures: (1) a catalogue of grapes; (2) a note from Dr. Pierre Magnol, in French.. [Corr., i. 449]

Persons encountered: William Charleton (letter), Dr. Pierre Magnol (enclosed note)

Tuesday, 3 January 1679, n.s.

24 December 1678, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Edward Bernard, dated 24 December 1678 o.s.. [Corr., i. 435]

Person encountered: Edward Bernard (letter)

Wednesday, 4 January 1679, n.s.

25 December 1678, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Jacques Selapris, dated 4 January 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 436]

Person encountered: Jacques Selapris (letter)

Thursday, 5 January 1679, n.s.

26 December 1678, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 26 December 1678 o.s.; written from Lincolns Inn Fields, London; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 437]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Saturday, 14 January 1679, n.s.

4 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letters:

  • From Jacques Horutener and Jacques Selapris, dated 14 January 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 438]
  • From Samuel Thomas, dated 4 January 1679 o.s.; written from Christ Church Oxford. [Corr., i. 439]
  • To Sir John Banks, dated 14 January 1679 n.s. (lost); answered by Banks on 20/30 January. [See Corr., i. 442]

Persons encountered: Sir John Banks (letter), Jacques Selapris (letter), Samuel Thomas (letter)

Sunday, 15 January 1679, n.s.

5 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: To Jacques Selapris, dated 15 January 1679 n.s. (lost); answered by Selapris on 12/22 January. [See Corr., i. 440]

Person encountered: Jacques Selapris (letter)

Sunday, 22 January 1679, n.s.

12 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Jacques Selapris, dated 22 January 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers a lost letter from Locke dated 5/15 January. [Corr., i. 440]

Person encountered: Jacques Selapris (letter)

Saturday, 28 January 1679, n.s.

18 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Thomas Herbert, dated 18 January 1679 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke at Paris; year from Locke’s endorsement; not received for some time. [Corr., i. 441]

Person encountered: Thomas Herbert, later earl of Pembroke (letter)

Monday, 30 January 1679, n.s.

20 January 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 20 January 1679 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “yours … of the 14th instant”. [Corr., i. 442]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)
