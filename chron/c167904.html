Wednesday, 1 April 1679, n.s.

22 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letters:

  • From Jacques Selapris, dated 1 April 1679 n.s.; written from Lyon; addressed to Locke at Paris. [Corr., i. 457]
  • From Dr. Denis Grenville, dated 1 April 1679 n.s.; written from Aix; answers “yours of the 14th of last month”. [Corr., i. 458]
  • From George Walls and Nathaniel Hodges, dated 22 March 1679 o.s.; written from Christ Church Oxford; addressed to Locke at Banks’s house at Lincolns Inn Fields, London. [Corr., i. 459]

Person encountered: Dr. Denis Grenville (letter), Nathaniel Hodges (letter), Jacques Selapris (letter), George Walls (letter)

Thursday, 6 April 1679, n.s.

27 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 27 March 1679 o.s.; addressed to Locke at Paris; preceded by a copy of Banks’s letter of 20 March, written by an amanuensis. [Corr., i. 460]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)

Monday, 10 April 1679, n.s.

31 March 1679, o.s.

[At Paris].

Letter: From Sir John Banks, dated 31 March 1679 o.s.; written from London; addressed to Locke at Paris; answers “yours of the 29th instant” (lost). [Corr., i. 460]

Person encountered: Sir John Banks (letter)
