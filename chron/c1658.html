1658

January | February | March | April | May | June
July | August | September | October | November | December

January

Saturday, 23 January 1658, o.s.

2 February 1658, n.s.

Letters:

  • To [William Carr], dated 23 January 1658; written at Christ Church Oxford; answered by Carr on 30 January. [De Beer, i. 37]
  • From John Strachey, dated 23 January 1648. [De Beer, i. 38]

Persons encountered: William Carr (letter); John Strachey (letter).

Saturday, 30 January 1658, o.s.

9 February 1658, n.s.

Letter: From William Carr, dated 30 January [1658]; year based on the content of the letter; answers Locke’s letter of 23 January. [De Beer, i. 39]

Person encountered: William Carr (letter)

March

Thursday, 18 March 1658, o.s.

28 March 1658, n.s.

Letter: From Thomas Grenfeild, dated 18 March 1658; written at Pensford. [De Beer, i. 40]

Person encountered: Thomas Grenfeild (letter)

Date Uncertain (late March?)

Letter: To “M. J.” [John Maggs?]; draft; possibly answered by Maggs ca. 1 April. [De Beer, i. 41]

Person encountered: M. J. [John Maggs?] (letter)

  ▲  

April

Thursday, 1 April 1658, o.s.

11 April 1658, n.s.

Letter: From John Maggs to [Locke?] [ca. 1 April 1658?]; possibly answer the letter Locke drafted in late March. [De Beer, i. 42]

Person encountered: John Maggs (letter).

Tuesday, 6 April 1658, o.s.

16 April 1658, n.s.

Letter: To John Locke, sen., dated (incorrectly) 6 February 1658; written at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 43]

Person encountered: John Locke, sen. (letter).

June

Tuesday, 29 June 1658, o.s.

9 July 1658, n.s.

Locke graduates Master of Arts at Oxford University. [De Beer, i. 44n.]

August

Sunday, 29 August 1658, o.s.

8 September 1658, n.s.

Locke’s birthday; age 26.

  ▲  

September

Tuesday, 14 September 1658, o.s.

24 September 1658, n.s.

Letter: From Isaiah Ward, dated 14 September [1658?]; written at Enfield; addressed to Locke at Christ Church Oxford. [De Beer, i. 44]

Person encountered: Isaiah Ward (letter).

Date Uncertain

Letter: To an unidentified lady, [1658/1659?]. [De Beer, i. 45]

  ▲  

Sources:
