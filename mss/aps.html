American Philosophical Society, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

Misc. MS. 1169

Letter from Locke to Nicolas Toinard, [10/]20 August 1678.

Superscription: Angers 20 août 78.

Salutation: Monsieur

Signature: Monsieur | Vostre très-humble et tres acquis serviteur | J Locke

Address: A Monsieur Monsieur Toynard a la teste moire dans la rüe Mazarine Fauxbourg St Germain a Paris.

In French; answered by Toinard on 17/27 August.

Copy in British Library, Add. MS. 28,836, ff. 1-2.

Printed: Catalogue of the Alfred Morrison Collection, 3 (1888):182-183; Corr., ix. 399*; (from the copy) Ollion (ed.), Notes sur la correspondance de John Locke (1908), pages 32-33; Ollion (ed.), Lettres inédites de John Locke à ses amis …, pages 17-18; Corr., i. 399.
