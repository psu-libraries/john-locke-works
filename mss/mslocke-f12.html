Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke f. 12

Pocket memorandum and account book, 1664-1672.

Locke wrote from both ends of the book. The front contains “Mem[oranda]” dated March 28, 1664-Lady Day 1672. The back of the book (pp. 262-226 rev.) contains accounts and rents from 1664 to 1672. Most of the material relates to Locke’s property in Somerset.

Small 8o. 130 × 84 mm. xii, 278 pages (pp. i-xii, 19-225, 263-278 blank; pp. 262-231 rev. numbered 1-31).
