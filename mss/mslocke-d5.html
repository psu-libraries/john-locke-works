Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke d. 5

Transcripts of two hermetical works, neatly written in the hand of an unknown scribe (1688).

There are no endorsements or other evidence connecting this manuscript to Locke. [Note: Long’s statement that the manuscript is in the hand of Sylvester Brounower is almost certainly incorrect.]

4o. 243 × 183 or smaller. A-K4L2. 46 leaves. Modern Bodleian binding.
