This index lists Locke’s correspondents in alphabetical order, followed by a list of letters. The listing is by the numbers assigned by E. S. de Beer, in The correspondence of John Locke (1976-89), and was compiled by merging the lists that appeared at the end of each of De Beer’s volumes.

The lower case roman numerals indicate the volume in the De Beer edition. Numbers in roman type indicate letters written by Locke; numbers in italic, those written to Locke; numbers in parentheses indicate letters between persons other than Locke or items other than letters.

This Index is the first step in registering Locke’s correspondence in John Locke Manuscripts. Gradually, information about each letter will be added.
