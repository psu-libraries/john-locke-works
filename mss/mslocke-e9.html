Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke e. 9

Three papers relating to Virginia

Working papers for the Board of Trade, 1697; in a modern binding, but the gatherings are signed by Locke in a single sequence, and each paper begins on the same page on which the previous one ends. The text is written on the recto pages only.

8o. 192 × 80 mm. A-I8. i, 71 leaves (ff. 68v-71 blank).


ff. 1-39:

“Some of the cheif greivances of the present constitution of Virginia, with an essay towards the remedies thereof”
