Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke e. 3

Catalogue of Locke’s library, transcribed by Sylvester Brownover in 1697 from MS. Locke f. 16. Additions acquired up to 1704 are entered by Locke on the left-hand (verso) page.

8o. 185 × 120 mm. π6 A B C18 C26 D-Z AA-CC8 DD6. pages [iii-xiv, 1-456].
