Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke c. 45

Letter of John Locke to Nicolas Toinard, 25 May 1679 (ff. 5-6), with six engraved portraits of Locke (fols. 4, 9-13). Purchased in 1961.

25 leaves (1-3, 14-25 blank). Bound in red leather with gilt tooling; with the title “John Locke, autograph letter” on the cover and “John Locke 1779” on the spine.
