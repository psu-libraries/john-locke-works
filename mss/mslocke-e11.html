Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke e. 11

A manuscript in an unknown hand, containing part of Daniel Whitby’s “The preface to the first Epistle to the Corinthians.” Printed (with some differences in text) in A paraphrase and commentary on the New Testament (1703), vol. 2:105-111. The manuscript was originally enclosed in Whitby’s letter to Locke, 1 Jan. 1699 [MS Locke c. 23, f. 94].

4o (not quired). 200 × 150 mm. 24 leaves (ff. 23v-24v blank; ff. 23-24 uncut at top). Modern Bodleian binding.
