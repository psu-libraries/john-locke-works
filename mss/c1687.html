Gravitas   [September?] 1687

Location:   Bodleian Library, MS. Locke c. 33, f. 20v.

Description:   A note with the caption “Gravitas” and initialed by Locke. Undated but occurs on a sheet dated September 1687.

Publications:   None

An inward inspiration or revelation   (December 1687)

Location:   Bodleian Library, MS. Locke c. 27, ff. 73-74.

Description:   Notes by Locke headed “An inward inspiration or revelation” and endorsed “Immediate ispiration [sic] Dec. 87”.

On a single sheet folded to form four pages, 335 × 210 mm; f. 74v is blank except for the endorsement and two drawings of heads (by Sylvester Brounower?).

Publications:

  1. “Testi teologico-filosofici lockiani dal MS. Locke c.27 della Lovelace Collection” / a cura di Mario Sina. // IN: Rivista di filosofia neo-scolastic. – 64 (1972):69-73.
  2. Writings on religion / John Locke ; edited by Victor Nuovo. – Oxford : Clarendon Press, 2002. – pp. 37-41 [Locke 867+]
  3. Theological manuscripts / John Locke ; edited by Victor Nuovo. – Oxford : Clarendon Press, to be published. – (The Clarendon edition of the works of John Locke) [Locke #961]

Discussions:   Sina, publication #1 above, pp. 68-69; Nuovo, publication #2 above, pp. xxxiii-xxxv.
