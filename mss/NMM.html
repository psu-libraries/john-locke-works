National Maritime Museum, Greenwich

LBK/8

p. 821:

Letter from Samuel Pepys to Caleb Banks, dated 29 August[/8 September] 1678.

Printed: Corr., ix. (405A).

p. 823:

Letter to Locke from Samuel Pepys, dated 29 August[/8 September] 1678.

Superscription: 29 August, 1678.

Copy in Pepys’s letter-book; original sent with a letter to Caleb Banks and a letter from Sir John Banks to Caleb.

Printed: Corr., ix. 405* (from the manuscript); Pepys, Further correspondence, 1662-1679, ed. J. R. Tanner (1929), page 320; Corr., i. 405 (from the printed copy).

p. 827:

Letter from Samuel Pepys to Caleb Banks, dated 30 September[/10 October] 1678.

Encloses letters from from Sir John to his son and from Sir John to Locke, all dated 30 September; discusses a plan for Caleb and Locke to travel to Italy.

Printed: Corr., ix. (410A).
