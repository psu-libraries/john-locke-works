Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke f. 34

A guardbook in which are mounted three unbound account books:

  1. Part of an account book of Sylvester Brounover, in which he entered payments made on Locke’s behalf in Holland, 1685-88 (ff. 1-16)
    Two unsigned quires of 8 leaves each, 165 × 105 mm.
  2. Account book used by Brounover for payments on Locke’s behalf, 1689-96 (ff. 9-68); inscribed by Locke “Syls Accounts” (f. 9)
    Six unsigned quires of eight leaves each and one quire of four leaves, 160 × 100 mm; pages 1-97 [98-104 blank]
  3. Account book used by William Shaw for payments on Locke’s behalf, 1701-1704.
    Three unsigned quires of eight leaves each, 155 × 100 mm; 1-29 [rest unnumbered; ff. 87v-92 blank]
