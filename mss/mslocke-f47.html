Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke f. 47

A bound volume in which Locke made a careful copy of an alphabetical list of the names of about 2000 authors of medical works. The list is written on the left (verso) page, in two rules columns (for forename and surname), with a few additional notes on the opposite rectos. The front flyleaf (f. i) bears Peter King’s signature and the dates “Anno Domini 1681” and “Anno Domini 1688” in an unknown hand.

8o. 147 × 97 mm. π6 A-K8 L6. vi, 86 leaves.
