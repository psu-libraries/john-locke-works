Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke c. 16

A guardbook in which are mounted letters to Locke from Damaris Cudworth (later Lady Masham), the brothers Sir Thomas and William Molyneux, the Earl and Countess of Monmouth, Sir Isaac Newton, John Norris, Locke’s London landlord Robert Pawling, and others.

244 leaves.
