British Library

Add. MS. 15,642

Locke’s Journal for 1679

8o. A-M8. 189, [3] pages. 158 × 107 mm. Signatures written by Locke at the bottom of the first page of each quire, together with the date “1679.” Pages numbered by Locke, with a few errors.

Bound with Ephemeris ov Almanach historial, povr l’an de grace, mil six cens septante-neuf. Le tout supputé iour par iour, suiuant le calcule de Tichobrahèm & suiuant la doctrine des anciens arrabes, qui ont escrit de cette science. Par Me. L. C., astrologue & mathematicien, disciple de M. Qvestier. Avec l’Amnanach dv Palais. A Troyes & se vendent à Paris, chez la V. Nicolas Ovdot, ruë veille Bouclerie. 12o. [A]8B4C8D4E6 [-E7-8?]. [31] leaves (unpaged)

Purchased by the British Museum 13 December 1845.

A microfilm copy is available in the Bodleian Library (MS. Film 424).

The following individual notes are included in this listing:

pp. 13-14 (20-21 February 1679):

“Atlantis”

p. 18 (20 February 1679):

“Carolina”

p. 22 (22 February 1679):

“Marriage”

p. 101 (17 June 1679):

“Opinion”

pp. 108-111 (3 July 1679):

“Unity”
