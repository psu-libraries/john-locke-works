Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke c. 4

A guardbook in which are mounted letters to Locke from Samuel Bold, Robert Boyle, Locke’s servant Sylvester Brounover, Leibniz’s correspondent Thomas Burnett of Kemney, and others.

216 leaves.
