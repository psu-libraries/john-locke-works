Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke c. 21

A guardbook in which are mounted letters to Locke from Nicolas Toinard, 1678-1704.

232 leaves.

Individual items in the volume include:

Toinard, Nicolas, 1628-1706.   [134 letters, 1678-1704]

ff. 1-2:

Letter from Toinard, [12/]22 July 1678.

Superscription: A Paris ce 22. Juill. 78.

Signature: Votre tres humble et tres obeissant serviteur | Toinard

Address: A Monsieur Monsieur Locke Gentilhomme Anglois demeurant aux trois Empereurs A Orleans

Endorsements: Mr Toinard 22 Jul. 78; Hugrometre; Pendul; Handmill; Gendron.

Answers letters from Locke dated 14 July and 21 July; answered by Locke on 26 July.

Printed: Corr., i. 392.

Endorsement: Mr Toinard 24 Jul. 78.

Answered by Locke on 16/26 July.

Printed: Corr., i. 393.
