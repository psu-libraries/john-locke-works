Passport issued to Sir Walter Vane for his diplomatic mission to Cleves  
[11 November 1665]

Location:   Bodleian Library, MS. Locke c. 25, ff. 8-9.

Description:   Passport for Sir Walter Vane and his train for the diplomatic mission to the Elector of Brandenburg at Cleves; signed by Sir William Morice, Secretary of State, 11 November 1665; endorsed “Sr William Vanes passeport” [sic].
A single sheet of paper folded to form 2 leaves; f. 8v and f. 9 are blank.

Publications:   None

Letterbook of Sir Walter Vane for his diplomatic mission to Cleves  
[11 November 1665]

Location:   British Library, Add. MS. 16,272.

Description:   Letterbook of Sir Walter Vane, during his embassy to the Elector of Brandenburgh at Cleves, from December 1665 to February 1666; written by Locke as secretary to the embassy. Contains copies of correspondence with officials in England, the ambassador’s proposals to the Elector, and the replies of the Elector’s commissioners. Includes (f. 4) an index of letters (not in Locke’s hand).

Folio. 320 × 27 mm. 59 leaves (including 3 preliminary leaves not part of the letterbook); ff. 4v-55 numbered by Locke pp. 1-102 (some versos not numbered); ff. 56-59 not numbered.

Publications:   None

Menstrua   [ca. 1665]

Location:   Bodleian Library, MS. Locke d. 9, p. 6.

Description:   A note in Locke’s hand, with the marginal caption “Menstrua”, and intialed by Locke. The note is undated, but was written ca. 1665.

Publications:   None

Pestis L   [ca. 1665]

Location:   Bodleian Library, MS. Locke d. 9, p. 8.

Description:   Several notes in Locke’s hand, with the marginal caption “Pestis L”, and intialed by Locke. The notes are undated, but was written ca. 1665, although they may not all be of the same date.

Publications:   None

Pleuritis   [ca. 1665]

Location:   Bodleian Library, MS. Locke d. 9, p. 9.

Description:   A note in Locke’s hand, with the marginal caption “Pleuritis”, and intialed by Locke. The note is undated, but was written ca. 1665; it is the first (and shortest) note on this topic on the same page.

Publications:   None
