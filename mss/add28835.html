British Library

Add. MS. 28,835

Letters from Locke to Nicolas Toinard, 1678-1700, and other miscellaneous papers. Collected by Jacques Charles Brunet. Acquired 1871.

Includes:

ff. 1-2:

Letter from Locke to Toinard, 20 May 1680.

[printed in Ollion (ed.), Notes sur la correspondance de John Locke (1908), pp. 68-72; and Ollion (ed.), Lettres inédites de John Locke à ses amis …, p. 47-52; De Beer, ii. 538]

ff. 3-4:

Letter from Locke to Toinard, 29 November 1680.

[printed in Ollion (ed.), Notes …, pp. 107-110; Ollion (ed.), Lettres …, p. 80-83; De Beer, ii. 596]

ff. 5-6:

Letter from Locke to Toinard, 31 May 1681.

[printed in Ollion (ed.), Notes …, pp. 132-135; Ollion (ed.), Lettres …, p. 102-104; De Beer, ii. 638]

ff. 7-8:

Letter from Locke to Toinard, 14 October 1681.

[printed in Ollion (ed.), Notes …, pp. 140-143; Ollion (ed.), Lettres …, p. 108-112; De Beer, ii. 665]

ff. 9-10:

Letter from Locke to Toinard, 25 June/5 July 1686.

[printed in Ollion (ed.), Lettres …, p. 118-119; De Beer, iii. 853]

ff. 11-12:

Letter from Locke to Toinard, 8 July 1700.

[printed in Ollion (ed.), Lettres …, p. 143-144; De Beer, vii. 2741]
