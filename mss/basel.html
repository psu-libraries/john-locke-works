University Library, Basel

Letter to John Strachey, 16 September [1673].

Superscription: Exeterhouse 16Sept:

Salutation: Sir

Signature: Sir | most affectionate humble servant | John Locke

Address: For John Strachy Esquire at Sutton Court. To be left at Dr Codringtons shop in High street Bristoll

Postmark: SE —

Year based on content of the letter.

Printed: De Beer, i. 276; Rand (ed.), Clarke correspondence (1927), page 80.
