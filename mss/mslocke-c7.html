MS. Locke c. 7

251 leaves.

Cooper, Anthony Ashley, Lord Ashley, later 1st Earl of Shaftesbury, 1621-1683   [6 letters, 1674-1681]

ff. 69-70:

Letter from Shaftesbury, 23 November [1674].

Superscription: St Giles Novemb the 23th

Salutation: Mr Locke

Signature: Deare Sir | Your truely affectionate freind and servant | Shaftesbury

Address: Thesse For his much esteemed Friend John Locke Esquire [by an amanuensis]

Endorsement: E: Shaftesbury 23o Nov. 74

Mostly written by an amanuensis; date from the content of the letter and Locke’s endorsement.

Printed: Christie, Shaftesbury 2:60-64; Corr., i. 297; Goldie (ed.), Selected correspondence, pages 49-51; incomplete in King, Life (1829), pages 33-36; excerpts in Bourne, Life, 1:277-278, 293, 332-333, 423.

f. 71:

Letter from Shaftesbury, 23 February[/5 March] 1677.

Date: Feb: 23d. 1676.

Salutation: Sir

Signature: Your most affectionate freind and servant | Shaftesbury

Address: Thesse To mr Locke [by a third hand]

Endorsement: E. Shaftesbury 23 Feb. 76/7

Probably forwarded by Sir John Banks on 26 Feb/8 Mar 1677.

Printed: Christie, Shaftesbury 2:235; Corr., i. 322.
