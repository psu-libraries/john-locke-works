Bodleian Library, Oxford University

MS. Locke c. 2

Locke’s ledger, 1701-1704. A bound volume, marked “Old Accts from 1700 to 1704” in the hand of Peter King. There is an alphabetical index of names and other account headings (p. ii-xxiii). After Locke’s death, Peter King turned the book around and began entering accounts at the back of the volume (pp. 339-338 rev.). The accounts are laid out and cross-referenced in the same manner as those in MS. Locke c. 1.

Folio. 308 × 200 mm. xxiv, 97, 98-342 pages (pp. 97-337 blank). Bound in vellum.
