Changes at Locke Studies

After 43 years as editor of The Locke Newsletter and Locke Studies, Roland Hall has stepped down as editor.

The new editor, beginning with volume 13 (2013), will be Timothy Stanton.

The new editor has created a new website for the journal: http://www.lockestudies.org/. Detailed information about the journal and its history may be found there, as well as the contents of all the issues, the full text of selected articles, and information on submitting articles and subscribing to the journal.
