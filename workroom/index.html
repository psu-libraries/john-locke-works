John Locke Bibliography

Workroom



By chance, you have wandered into my workroom. Here I keep the working files I use to maintain the John Locke Bibliography.

These files contain almost 9000 entries for works by or about John Locke, from the 17th century to the present. Only a fraction of these entries are included in the World Wide Web version of the bibliography. The indexes here are not terribly informative but, if you happen upon anything that interests you, feel free to ask me for the full citation.
